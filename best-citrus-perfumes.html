Best Lemon-Forward Perfume

Maison Margiela 'REPLICA' Under the Lemon Trees

1

Best Lemon-Forward Perfume

Maison Margiela 'REPLICA' Under the Lemon Trees

Fresh lemon is balanced by coriander and musk in this transportive, sophisticated fragrance.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

“One of my absolute favorite perfumes! A fresh crowd pleaser perfect for everyday wear during the spring and summer! I get a lot of the green tea and céder the longer it stays on my skin. Highly recommend!” —worduser

Scent NotesKalmansi Accord, Coriander, Musk
Size1 fl oz.

2

Best Not-Too-Citrus Perfume

KAYALI Citrus

2

Best Not-Too-Citrus Perfume

KAYALI Citrus

This slightly sweet option is citrus forward, but it's blended with a good bit of rose for added floral depth.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

“I love how the scent starts with that bright and fresh citrusy blast. After a few minutes, it starts to settle into a gentle rose. Rose can be a powerful and strong element in a perfume but here, it is tamed by the musk.” —loladiva

Scent NotesBergamot, Pink Grapefruit, Rose Centifolia
Size3.4 oz/100 mL

Best Clean Citrus Perfume

Henry Rose Windows Down

3

Best Clean Citrus Perfume

Henry Rose Windows Down

This blend from clean line Henry Rose has a handful of citrus notes—neroli, grapefruit, bergamot, and orange blossom—but still has a light and airy quality.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

“I'm closing in on 50 and this is the perfume I have been looking for all my life. It smells fresh and not like chemicals. It makes you want to stop and take a deep breath. It does not give me a headache and it makes me happy to put it on in the morning.” —Kellie

Size50 mL
Scent NotesTop: Neroli, Grapefruit, Bergamot, Earl Grey Tea Accord Middle: Orange Flower, Jasmine Bottom: Moss, Guaiacwood, Musk

10

Best Sophisticated Citrus Perfume

Matiere Premiere Neroli Oranger

10

Best Sophisticated Citrus Perfume

Matiere Premiere Neroli Oranger

Matiere Premiere leaves no room for mystery; every ingredient in this fragrance has a clearly defined natural origin: orange blossom from Tunisia, bergamot from Italy, neroli from Lebanon, and ylang ylang from Comoros.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

“Nice fresh and uplifting neroli/orange blossom! To my nose this has the perfect amount of astringency; enough to add a refreshing vibe but not enough to make it unpalatable. Excellent combination of sweet, fresh, and clean. Great for a hot day in spring or summer!” —wst

Size100 ml
Scent NotesTop: Neroli, Bergamot Middle: Orange blossom Base: Ylang Ylang Musk

Best Grapefruit-Forward Perfume

Jo Malone London Grapefruit Cologne

11

Best Grapefruit-Forward Perfume

Jo Malone London Grapefruit Cologne

Now 15% Off

It may say grapefruit on the bottle, but this simple—but never boring—scent has notes of rosemary, peppermint, vetiver, and more to round out its fresh finish.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

“​​This scent, by far, is the best I've ever worn. Some scents spray on strong and you need to wait for it to ‘settle’ to enjoy its fragrance. You will enjoy its fragrance from the second you spray it on.” —rcc1022

Size1 fl oz
Scent NotesTop: grapefruit, tangerine. Middle: jasmine, pimento, peppermint, rosemary. Base: patchouli, vetiver, moss

Best Vacation-Ready Perfume

AERIN Limone di Sicilia Parfum

12

Best Vacation-Ready Perfume

AERIN Limone di Sicilia Parfum

This sweet lemon scent will hold you over until you make that White Lotus-inspired Sicily trip happen.

What the Reviewers Are Saying:

“Like being transported to Italy. Wearing this fragrance reminds me of a walk through a grove of lemon trees, a limoncello store, or walking on a stone street on a sunny day with a gelato al limone. Instant love.” —Rochelle

Limited Edition

Aerin Mediterranean Honeysuckle Clementina

13

Limited Edition

Aerin Mediterranean Honeysuckle Clementina

Speaking of dreamy Italian vacations, Aerin just released a new sister scent to its ultra-popular Mediterranean Honeysuckle, called Mediterranean Honeysuckle Clementina. 14

Best Spicy Citrus Perfume

Acqua di Parma Yuzu Eau de Parfum

Japanese yuzu gets the star treatment in this perfume, which also features notes of sandalwood and licorice.
