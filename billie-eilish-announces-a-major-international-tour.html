Billie Eilish Announces A Major International Tour

 Posted on April 7, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1028 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Billie Eilish announced that she’s prepping a tour in support of her forthcoming third studio album, with dates scheduled at arenas across North America, Europe, the UK, and Australia.

Produced by Live Nation, Eilish’s Hit Me Hard And Soft: The Tour kicks off at Centre Videotron in Quebec on September 29th, with the North American segment wrapping with a three-show run at the Kia Forum in Los Angeles, ending on December 17.

Eilish then heads across the Pacific for a run of performances in Australia, including shows at the Brisbane Entertainment Centre, Rod Laver Arena, and Qudos Bank Arena.

European dates start on April 23rd when Eilish is scheduled to take the stage at Avicii Arena in Stockholm and concludes at Palau Sant Jordi in Barcelona on June 15.

UK Shows start at the OVO Hydro in Glasgow on July 7th and wrap with two shows at Dublin’s 3Arena on July 26th and 27th.

Tickets for the tour will go on sale starting with the American Express® Presale beginning Tuesday, April 30, with additional presales running throughout the week.
