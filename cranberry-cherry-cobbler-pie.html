Cranberry-Cherry Cobbler Pie

 Posted on February 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  185 words  |  Larita Shotwell
Brian Woodcock

This dessert is two in one: cobbler and pie at the same time!

Yields: 8

Prep Time: 25 mins

Total Time: 1 hr 25 mins

Ingredients

Save Recipe
  • 1/4 c. cornstarch
  • 1 tsp. finely grated lime zest
  • 1 tbsp. lime juice
  • 2/3 c. plus 2 tbsp. sugar, divided, plus more for sprinkling
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt, divided
  • 12 oz. cranberries
  • 12 oz. frozen sweet cherries
  • 1 1/2 c. all-purpose flour, spooned and leveled
  • 2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 c. cold unsalted butter, cut up
  • 3/4 c. buttermilk or whole milk

Directions

  • Step 1Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Whisk together cornstarch, lime zest, lime juice, 2/3 cup sugar and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Add cranberries and cherries; toss to combine. Transfer to a 9-inch pie plate.
  • Step 2Whisk together flour, baking powder, 2 tablespoons sugar, and remaining salt. Cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse meal. Add buttermilk and gently mix to form a wet, shaggy dough. Dollop mounds of dough on top of fruit; sprinkle with sugar. Bake, on baking sheet, until golden and bubbling, 50 to 55 minutes; cool slightly.
