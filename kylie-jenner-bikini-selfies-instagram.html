Kylie Jenner Ushers In Summer in Sizzling Poolside Bikini Selfies

 Posted on August 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  304 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

At last, we’re approaching the end of winter…and entering the start of summer? Kylie Jenner seems eager to skip seasons and get to the good part of the year, judging from her latest salvo of sizzling Instagram selfies.

On Instagram, the Kylie Cosmetics mogul recently shared a poolside photo of herself soaking up the sun, reclining in a white lounge chair. With her hair damp and slightly slicked back, she looked unbothered by the calendar month in a classic black triangle bikini. She accessorized her sun-kissed look minimally, simply wearing a dainty gold Cartier Trinity bracelet.

Instagram

Jenner continued showing off her outfit with a mirror selfie posted to her Instagram Story. In that snap, she wore black biker shorts, which she rolled down to reveal a sliver of her bikini bottoms, and a black net bag thrown over her shoulder. In one hand, she carried a rainbow-colored crochet sun hat.

Instagram

In spite of the cold weather, Jenner has still been able to sartorially strike out on her own, with a slate of best-dressed Fashion Month looks over the past few weeks

Earlier this month, she put a spin on the viral mob wife trend by wearing a massive caramel Nereja fur coat—which she wore with seemingly nothing underneath. Instead, Jenner paired the statement outerwear with blue straight-leg jeans from Levi’s, a red and blue Nike duffel bag, ’70s-era Metzler Zeiss eyeglasses with a black rim, and black pointed-toe stiletto boots from the Row.
