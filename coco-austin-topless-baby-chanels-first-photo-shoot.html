Coco Austin's daughter Chanel Nicole may only be a week old, but the infant has already spent some serious time behind the camera -- and the pics are beyond adorable.

The first time mom took to Instagram on Sunday and shared a video with her followers of the tiny baby at the shoot, where it looked like little Chanel was already a pro at having her photos taken!
