The Bridesmaids actress teamed the ensemble with a pair of colourful Nike runners and carried her belongings in a cross-body black handbag.

Her stunning make-up free face was partially concealed beneath a pair of over-sized sunglasses, while her carefree brunette locks fell around her shoulders.

Window shopping: The loving couple, both dressed in casual chic, stopped and look through stores on their romantic stroll 

Bobby, 45, looked the epitome of winter cool in a grey beanie, a black zip up jacket and navy jeans.  

He finished off his casual look with a pair of comfortable black sneakers and sunglasses.

During their walk, the Aussie beauty and her Third Watch beau made sure to stop and look through New York's famous shops, including ABC Carpet & Home store. 

Wait: Bobby hailed down a cab for the duo during the outing 

While the couple are yet to confirm Rose's pregnancy, US Weekly reported in mid-October that the actress and her long time beau were expecting their first child together.

The pair, who have been dating for three years after meeting on the set of the Annie reboot in 2012, recently snapped up a three-bedroom house in Brooklyn, New York for an estimated $2.2 million.

Meanwhile, Rose, who has been filming the sequel to Bad Neighbours with Seth Rogen and Zac Efron, recently said she feels incredibly 'lucky' to share her life with her partner.

'He's this very kind-hearted, wonderful guy. I'm very lucky to have met him,' she gushed.

The pair star together in both Spy and Adult Beginners, and Rose revealed she was initially 'nervous' about working with her boyfriend, but is happy the risk has paid off.

'We're getting to work together a lot lately. I was initially nervous - clearly, it could have not been a good idea - but it's fun to have someone close to you on set rather than spending months apart,' she said.
