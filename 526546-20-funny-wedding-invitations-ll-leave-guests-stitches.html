20 funny wedding invitations that'll leave your guests in stitches

 Posted on April 25, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1172 words  |  Aldo Pusey

An invitation sets the tone for the wedding because it is the first thing your guests will see. It gives the guests a glimpse of what they should expect on the big day. If you want your wedding invitation to stand out, you can add a touch of humour to it. Funny wedding invitations will lighten the mood and make your guests smile.

When designing your wedding invitations, you need to ensure that they are not as boring as the email you receive from your boss. After all, it is your big day, and you have to do everything possible to make your guests feel happy and excited. For instance, you can add hilarious phrases to the invites to make your guests look forward to the big day with excitement.

Funny wedding invitations for second marriage

When designing a wedding invitation for a second marriage, there are several things that you need to consider. But, if you want your guests to enjoy the invitation, you can incorporate these funny lines to showcase your sense of humour.

  • We no longer let our differences come in the way of our love. To celebrate this love, we wish to invite you to our second wedding that will be held on [date] at [venue].
  • We're getting wrapped up for the second time! Come and celebrate with us at [venue] on [date].
  • This is an invitation to you and your family to pull my ear personally for all those mistakes. Please join us as we get married for the second time...because it won't be a wedding without your blessings.
  • We've got a new reason to be madder! We're getting married for the second time. Be a part of this madness on [date] at [venue]. Please come and shower us with your blessings!
  • We managed to preserve our love for so long. Now, we're finally unravelling! Please join us on this happy occasion and shower us with your blessings. The wedding celebration will take place on [date] at [venue].
  • Since he has understood the golden rule that keeps us going, we've decided to give love a second chance. We're getting married on [date] at [venue]. Please be part of this special occasion.

Funny wedding invitations for friends

Your wedding day is the ideal opportunity to keep that humour rolling with your friends. If you are struggling with how to go about the funny wedding invitations wording, the following lines should serve as an inspiration.

  • With the grace of God, we are tying the knot on (date) at (time). Join us as we say goodbye to the fun times and hello to married life!
  • We're tying the knot, and there's an open bar — what else do you need to know? We request your presence to be a part of this holy ritual, witnessed by the Almighty, as we become one.
  • We know how much you love free booze and food. Why else do you think we're doing this? Save the date for the biggest party ever.
  • Now it's time I officially invite you to join us as we celebrate my biggest promotion! I've been promoted from the post of a boyfriend to a husband. Please come and be a part of this off-site celebration on [date] at [venue].
