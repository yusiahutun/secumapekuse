10 times Instagram hot monk Paing Takhon sent hearts racing by heating up regular careers

 Posted on March 2, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  456 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Paing began modelling in 2014 and has featured in numerous commercials, TV shows, cover shoots and music videos across Myanmar and Thailand.

He also apparently has a penchant for cosplaying other careers. Here are 10 more times we swooned over the model.

Covid-era businessman

Paing elevates a standard white dress shirt and black trousers combination with slick shoes and accessories, nailing the “salary man rushing to the office amid the pandemic” look. “Hard working man,” gushed one follower.

Chef

If anyone can make a toque blanche look attractive, it’s Paing Takhon. Rocking a white chef uniform, he asked in the photo’s caption, “What do you want to eat?” A flurry of thirsty answers that had absolutely nothing to do with farm-raised produce inevitably ensued.

Rancher

Speaking of farm-raised produce, the 1.88-metre-tall (six-foot, two-inch) Paing would also make a rather dishy organic ranch farmer. Here he is tending to the day’s chores.

Art teacher

One of Paing’s many talents is painting; he’s showcased his work multiple times online. But we’re probably not the only ones dreaming of an art class taught by the man himself.

Uber driver

If Uber drivers looked like this, other forms of transport would go out of business. Paing looks every bit the professional chauffeur of an Uber Lux, where we’d need to be told twice to get out of the front seat.

Street vendor

Fans swooned over this image of Paing working a street cart, captioned in Thai, “Papaya salad – delicious and tempting?” It’s definitely not just the papaya salad that some viewers were tempted by.

Thai boxing instructor

In 2019, Paing was appointed Myanmar’s tourism ambassador to Thailand, so he tied his locks up in a bun and tried his hand at Thai boxing. Though it’s unclear if he continued training, he’d surely have no problems finding students to join him in the ring.

Dog walker

A devoted animal-lover, Paing’s Instagram account is filled with the cutest puppies, kittens and even baby rhinos – enough to melt the coldest of hearts. If he wasn’t currently studying psychology, or never embarked on his career as an actor and model, he’d probably make a killing running a successful dog walking business.

Web designer

Born on the border of millennial and Gen Z (1996) Paing is a bit of a tech wizard. He was even hired by mobile phone company Oppo as their brand ambassador. This is what he’d probably look like working from home as a web designer.

Photographer

Paing would probably make a wonderful wedding photographer – he owns a number of cameras and has spent plenty of time on set. Of course, many might prefer it when he turns the camera around.
