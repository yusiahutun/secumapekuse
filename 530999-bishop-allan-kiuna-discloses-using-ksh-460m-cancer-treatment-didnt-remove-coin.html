Bishop Allan Kiuna Discloses Using KSh 460m for Cancer Treatment Abroad: &quot;Didn't Remove One Coin&quot;

 Posted on July 13, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  415 words  |  Martina Birk
  • Bishop Allan Kiuna turned 57 in style, and his wife threw him a party turned thanksgiving service to celebrate his full healing
  • The man of God had been battling cancer, and he stayed in the US for months as he underwent treatment
  • While addressing the congregants and dignitaries who were present for his all-white party, Kiuna revealed he spent KSh 460 million on treatment

Jubilee Christian Church founder Bishop Allan Kiuna is back in the country after months in the United States, where he was seeking cancer treatment.

The man of God was given a royal welcome, which also marked his 57th birthday, and he and his wife had dignitaries attend the thanksgiving service.

How Allan Kiuna paid for costly treatment

While saying hello to the congregants, Kiuna gave his testimony, where he thanked God for healing him fully, as he is now cancer-free.

He also disclosed how much his treatment cost, leaving many celebrating God for the miracle upon their church leader.

"For the one year that I was in the US, my treatment cost 3 million dollars (KSh 460 million). And I did not remove one coin from my pocket because the God of heaven provided," he said.

Kiuna also prophesied that the country's economy would also prosper, saying that things that were on the news were only casting fear on people.

His wife, Reverend Kathy Kiuna, has been holding forte in church weeks after coming from the US, where she was to support her husband as he underwent treatment.

Inside Kathy, Kiuna's anniversary

The religious couple, known for their flashy lifestyle, in December 2022 celebrated 28 years of marriage.

Kathy posted a photo they took of them kissing at their church's pulpit and pampering her husband with love.
