Lindsay Lohan and Sister Ali Make Rare Joint Appearance in Contrasting Looks

 Posted on February 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  311 words  |  Martina Birk

Lindsay Lohan is sharing the spotlight with her little sister Aliana Lohan.

Yesterday, the two had a busy day in New York City and showed off not one but two sets of complementing looks.

Lindsay, 36, and Ali, 28, were seen looking breezy in pastel dresses after an appearance on The Drew Barrymore Show. Ali wore a halter-top peach number with teal pumps, while Lindsay looked fresh in a powder blue sundress with white heels. Both accessorized with diamond jewelry.

James Devaney//Getty Images

Also that day, the duo enjoyed Manhattan together in chic contrasting ensembles: Lindsay looked striking in a black floor-length dress and matching pumps and bag, while Ali was all class in a plunging long-sleeved white minidress with an asymmetric detail and bright red heels.

Lindsay and Ali held hands as they posed for photos.

Gotham//Getty Images

Ali—one of four Lohan siblings—also accompanied her big sister to the screening of her Christmas flick Falling for Christmas on Wednesday. Ali actually makes an appearance alongside her famous sister in the movie, and also wrote one of the songs in its soundtrack, "Without You."

"My Single Without You coming November 10th to Netflix's Falling For Christmas starring @lindsaylohan Thank you with all my heart to @jasonmater @bedebk @jamesburnett @jaworob @alexkovacs @dinalohan and @lindsaylohan for making this happen," Ali captioned an Instagram post announcing the drop of her song and the premiere of the movie.

"❤️❤️❤️❤️ beautiful song! Beautiful talent! Beautiful woman!" Lindsay commented in the post, supporting her sister.
