Amanda Holden strips totally naked to parade ageless figure as she turns 50

 Posted on March 4, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  493 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Amanda Holden marked a milestone birthday this Tuesday, celebrating her 50th birthday with her most flamboyant photoshoot yet.

Taking place in a brightly-lit studio, the Britain's Got Talent star posed atop a white tiered prop, which had been strewn with pink peonies and roses.

Posing in the buff, Amanda dangled a cherry in her mouth and gazed at the camera, her blonde hair falling past her shoulders.

Hinting her birthday celebrations had gone to plan, in her caption, she added the shoot had been the "cherry on top of a fabulous day".

Amanda spent the special occasion with her family of four – husband Chris Hughes and their two daughters, Lexi, 15, and Hollie, nine.

Even though her family spoiled her "rotten" with gifts, Amanda plans to mark the occasion properly with a blowout bash once the lockdown lifts.

Entering the new decade in her life with a smile, the Heart FM host took the opportunity to reflect on the past 50 years, admitting "time flies".

Reaching out to her fans, she wrote: "How did This happen! Half a century.. time flies. . Thank you for all your kind & loving messages today.. my husband Chris and my gorgeous girls have spoiled me rotten and it's still going.

"My friends have sent the most hilarious messages & presents and made this day extra special ..I can't wait to celebrate in a HUGE way when we can."

The charismatic TV personality went on to promise her birthday celebrations would be a year-long event, predicting parties and gatherings galore.

She added: "I'm 50 all year and all year we're gonna party. Hang on to your loved ones. It's nearly over."

The family celebration comes days after Amanda's Heart FM team arranged a special party for the presenter in their London offices.

Complete with prosecco and a decorative wall of flowers, Amanda was suitably dazzled by the extravagant party planning.

Sharing some behind-the-scene snaps with fans on the day, she said: "Celebrating my birthday early with my Heart family.

"So grateful and overwhelmed at the thought and love they've put into it. #50 of my favourite things! Prosecco at 630am."

The BGT judge was in for another surprise on the airwaves when her former co-star Piers Morgan appeared to send her a special birthday message.

During their chat, Piers quipped: "I am loving your work in this pandemic, Amanda Holden. A, it's lovely to see you with some clothes on today."

As quick as could be, Amanda soon one-upped him by angling the camera to her bust and replying: "It's low-cut though, look!"
