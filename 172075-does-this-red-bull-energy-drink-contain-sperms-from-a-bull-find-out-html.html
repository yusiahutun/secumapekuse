- Is the organic acid - taurine - found in Red Bull energy drink extracted from a bull or man-made?

Do energy drinks have contain sperm from a bull?

Well, this is a question that seems to be impossible to put to rest ever since a study apparently found the drinks to contain elements.

Longhorn cattle company is said to have tested top energy drink brands (like Monster and Red Bull) and found that they contain bull sperm.

The bull sperm narrative was propelled by the fact that Red Bull has taurine, an organic acid, as part of its ingredients.

However, Red Bull dismissed the said study.
