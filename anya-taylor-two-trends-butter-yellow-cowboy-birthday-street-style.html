Anya Taylor-Joy Leans Into Two Different Trends With Her Birthday Street Style

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  345 words  |  Larita Shotwell

While it can be hard to find Anya Taylor-Joy not dressed in Dior, the Dune 2 actor did switch it up with a couple of looks she wore to stroll through New York City on Tuesday, which also happened to be her 28th birthday. And how exactly did she observe the occasion, fashion-wise? By leaning into two current trends.

It started when the Golden Globe winner was spotted in Midtown with her husband, Malcolm McRae, where she radiated Americana in a white tank, which she tucked into light-wash, high-waisted jeans and accentuated with two pops of leather: a motorcycle jacket that she draped across her shoulders and a pair of backless Tabis from Maison Margiela.

Gotham//Getty Images

However, it was her hat that really caught our attention—a butter-yellow baseball cap with the U.S. Open logo on it. Everyone from Jennifer Lawrence to Emily Ratajkowski has been embracing the trending color this spring, and it appears Taylor-Joy wanted to get on board, too.

But that wasn’t the only fad the Queen’s Gambit star embraced yesterday. Later, as she and McRae took a stroll throughout the city, Taylor-Joy winked at the current cowboy trend, which has also been everywhere, thanks in no small part to Beyoncé and her new album, Cowboy Carter.

The Image Direct

For this look, Taylor-Joy wore a baby tee from 12th Tribe with “I ♥️ Cowboys” across the chest in pink rhinestones. In contrast to the feminine tee, she continued with the leather touches from earlier in the day, this time in the form of a pair of knee-high black boots, a black miniskirt, and a matching leather jacket that she wrapped around her waist. To really sell the coolness of the look, Taylor-Joy accessorized with a pair of black cat-eye sunglasses.
