Savers Daily Morning Routine Morning Tips

You know those people who seem absolutely in love with the first half of the day? The early birds who are perfectly calm, organized, and fully awake without coffee, and never leave their packed lunch sitting on the kitchen counter as they rush out the door? It turns out, they could have a trick up their sleeves that you might to borrow if you'd like to start your day off on the right foot. 

What Is It?

It's called the SAVERS routine, and it was developed by Hal Elrod, author ofThe Miracle Morning. SAVERS stands for: silence, affirmations, visualization, exercise, reading, and scribing.

How Does It Help?

Once this daily practice becomes habit, you'll find that it:

· Puts you in a positive mental state

· Bolsters confidence

· Keeps your goals top-of-mind

· Increases your energy

· Kick starts your brain

· Encourages self-reflection

For more information and a handy infographic on the technique, check out author and entrepreneur James Altucher's blog post and interview with Elrod, titled How to Win the Day.
