Jadon Sancho | Early Life & Family

Jadon Malik Sancho was born to his parents, Sean Sancho and an unnamed mother, on March 25, 2000, in Camberwell, London.

Besides that, there is no information about his siblings, either. While growing up, Sancho was a die-hard Chelsea fan as they were from the same area.

On top of that, he idolized Chelsea legend and current manager Frank Lampard during his early years.

However, he joined the Watford academy as Chelsea’s academy was a bit too far to be traveling daily.

Thereafter, he remained with Watford until the age of 14 when Manchester City came in and swooped the young prodigy.

Subsequently, he remained on the south side of the City until 2017 before moving to Borrusia Dortmund. The reason is a lack of playing time under manager Pep Guardiola.

Jadon Sancho | Career & Stats

As of now, Jadon has only played for Borussia Dortmund as a professional. Likewise, he made his debut on October 21, 2017, against Eintracht Frankfurt.

After that, he played in a further 12 games scoring one goal.

Although he did not score many goals, his performances were so brilliant that he warranted a starting place in the Dortmund side the next season.

Subsequently, Jadon lifted his game with more playing time as he scored 13 goals in 41 appearances for the club.

Thus having had an excellent 2018-19 season, everyone expected the England international to kick on and become even better.

And staying true to everyone’s expectations, Sancho has scored an impressive 17 goals and provided a further 19 assists in just 35 games this season.

As a result, Premier League clubs Manchester United and Chelsea are vying for the signature of the talented player.
