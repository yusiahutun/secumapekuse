The Duchess of Sussex wears her failsafe spring uniform

 Posted on April 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  281 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

When it comes to her wardrobe, the Duchess of Sussex has a failsafe uniform that she has stuck to throughout her time in the public eye, and she still never diverts far from it. In her latest look – in which she was pictured in Palm Beach with husband Harry and their friends, Nacho Figueras and Delfina Blaquier – Meghan wore one of her favourite outfit formulas, wide-legged trousers and a button-up shirt.

In a number of pictures, where the couple could be seen filming for one of their new Netflix reality shows, Meghan was spotted sharing an embrace with her friend, and looking impossibly chic in her casual but put-together outfit.

The look consisted of a baggy blue shirt, which she wore untucked with a pair of white wide-legged trousers, statement sunglasses by Heidi Merrick and white Hermés Oran sandals (of which she owns multiple pairs).

Throughout her time in the spotlight, from her Suits days and during her time working as a member of the British royal family, Meghan has worn this outfit formula time and time again. In fact, she has favoured relaxed tailoring and buttoned-up shirts probably more than anything else.

Earlier in the weekend, Meghan attended a polo match, where she was on hand to support husband Harry, and presented him with the trophy when his team won.

For the occasion, she delivered once again in the fashion stakes, choosing a white dress by Heidi Merrick, vintage gold Chanel earrings, a Valentino bag, nude pumps by Aquazzura and some beautiful gold Cartier jewellery, including a Love Bangle and Princess Diana's Tank watch, which Harry gifted to her.
