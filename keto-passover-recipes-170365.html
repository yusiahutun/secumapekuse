The Best Keto Passover Recipes

 Posted on April 13, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  177 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Although people who observe Passover do not eat leavened bread for seven days, the occasion is often celebrated with carb-y dishes like matzo ball soup. If you’re on the keto diet (it’s best not to take a cheat day), you’ll be happy to know there are plenty of low-carb recipes that taste just as delicious as the classics you grew up eating on this Jewish holiday. 

From a savory appetizer to a tasty dessert, recipe developer (and keto follower) Chelsea Cohen of SlimFast, created a host of decadent dishes that are sure to excite anyone who sits down at your Passover table — whether they’re on the keto diet or not. And no, you’re not dreaming, it really does include a keto-friendly matzo ball soup!

Remember: Always talk to your doctor before trying any new diet, especially a restrictive one like keto.
