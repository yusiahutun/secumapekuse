Meet Beatrice Jean Howard-Gabel 

Beatrice Jean Howard-Gabel was born on January 19, 2012, in the United States of America. She follows the Christian faith and has a mixed Jewish, Germanic, and British heritage. 

The diversity of Beatrice’s ancestry isn’t the only exciting thing about her family. What truly sets her family apart is that it boasts several Hollywood stars. For instance, Beatrice is the granddaughter of Ronald William Howard, a highly acclaimed American film director, producer, and actor. Moreover, her aunts, Paige Howard and Jocelyn Howard, are also established Hollywood actresses.

Despite Beatrice’s family’s popularity, her parents prefer to keep her personal life private. As a result, the name and location of her school are unknown, among other personal details.

However, despite knowing the gender, Beatrice’s parents chose to keep it a surprise until her birth. Beatrice’s mother also discussed her struggles with severe postpartum depression after giving birth to her first child. During an interview in 2018, Bryce revealed that due to her horrifying experience, she was initially scared that the same problem would happen after Beatrice’s birth. However, she found solace in knowing she had her family and friends’ support.

A Little More About Beatrice’s Parents

Beatrice’s dad, Seth Gabel, is an American actor born on October 3, 1981, known for his roles in television shows like Salem and Nip/Tuck. He is also the grand-nephew of actor Martin Gabel. Seth was raised in Hollywood, Florida, as part of a Jewish family and took on the surname of his stepfather, Cosentino, who adopted him.

He completed his high school education at the University School of Nova Southeastern University in Davie, Florida, in 1999. Later, he attended New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts. It was during his youth that he became best friends with Josh Gad.

Beatrice’s mom, Bryce, was born in March 1981 in Los Angeles. She has gained recognition for her roles in films such as The Help, Rocketman, and the Jurassic World franchise. Recently, Bryce transitioned into directing and worked on several episodes of the popular Disney+ series, The Mandalorian.

Beatrice’s parents met at New York University’s Tisch School of the Arts. What initially started as a friendship blossomed into a deep and meaningful romance. After nurturing their bond for five years, the pair decided to take their relationship to the next level and get married.

They exchanged vows and tied the knot on June 17, 2006, in Greenwich, Connecticut. On February 16, 2007, Howard and Gabel welcomed their first child, Theodore Norman Howard-Gabel. Like his sister, most of his life is kept from the public eye.
