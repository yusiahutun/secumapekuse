Cardi B target of gang threats after dissing LA Crips

 Posted on April 16, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  430 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Cardi B found herself the target of gang threats on Tuesday after dissing the notorious LA Crips on Instagram.

The 25-year-old, who claims to have been a member of the rival Bloods since she was 16, threw shade by wearing a certain shade.

The rapper posted a pic of herself wearing a fur coat of blue — famously the preferred color of the Crips — but it was her caption that really poked the bear.
