Charles Ouda: Video of Actor Singing Passionately Resurfaces after His Tragic Death

 Posted on February 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  363 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • A video of actor Charles Ouda singing a soothing song has reemerged, showing his talent and spirit
  • The video captured Ouda in high spirits and singing passionately as someone recorded him using a phone
  • Many netizens have expressed their condolences and noted that the fallen actor had a premonition of his death

The Kenyan entertainment industry is mourning the death of veteran actor Charles Ouda, who passed away on Saturday, February 3, 2024.

Ouda was a versatile and passionate actor who featured in many local TV shows such as Makutano Junction, Pepeta and Salem.

He mentored many young and upcoming actors through acting workshops and classes.

Ouda’s death came as a shock to many of his fans and colleagues, who remembered him as a cheerful and talented person.

Charles Ouda - talented singer

As Kenyans pay tribute to him, a video of him singing passionately has reemerged on social media, showing a different side of his artistic abilities.

The video, which was shared by Sonia Ouko on TikTok, captured Ouda in high spirits and singing a soothing song as someone recorded him using a phone.

The one-minute video revealed that Ouda was not only a skilled actor but also a gifted singer who could use his tenor voice to sing and brighten other people’s faces.

The video has since gone viral, attracting massive reactions from netizens who expressed their condolences and admiration for Ouda.
