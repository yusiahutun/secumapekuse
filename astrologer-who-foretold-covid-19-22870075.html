Astrologer who 'foretold Covid-19' makes more predictions including on Meghan and Harry

 Posted on February 29, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  373 words  |  Aldo Pusey

An astrologer who “foretold coronavirus” has now made predictions for the next six months.

Jessica Adams made a claim that a virus would “disrupt the world” in February 2019.

She even even pinpointed when the first victim died in Wuhan, which was on January 10.

So what does the horoscopes expert have to say about Christmas and beyond?

In an interview with FEMAIL, Jessica spoke about Covid-19, the Royal Family, the US election and Brexit.

Here were her premonitions…

Coronavirus

Jessica doesn’t have much good news when it comes to coronavirus.

She believes we’ll have to “learn to live” with Covid-19 and doesn’t think it’s going anywhere anytime soon.

The Royal Family

After Megxit in January, Prince Harry and Meghan Markle left the UK.

There have been fears of a royal rift ever since – and Jessica doesn’t think this will heal in the next six months.

She predicted: “There will be no reunion between Princes William and Harry.

“The Sussexes will invest time in a radical new farming and food venture.

“The product will be an alternative to coffee, chocolate or meat and the businessman involved is rather handsome.”

The US Election

Jessica doesn’t think Donald Trump will win at the polls.

She predicts a move from Republican values to a more “feminist” leader who will push for greater equality.

Christmas

Good news – the astrologer thinks we’ll have a merry Christmas!

Apparently, there are positive astrological cycles taking place at Yuletide that will bring us all together.

She explained: “Things begin to change about three days before Christmas Eve — we see cycles we haven’t seen in decades.

“It’s going to be all about friendship and the group and global security.”
