Naomi Osaka biography

The professional tennis player was born on 16 October 1997 in Chuo-ku, Osaka Japan to Tamaki Osaka and Leonard Francois. Naomi Osaka parents come from different races.

Her mother is a native of Hokkaido in Japan while Naomi Osaka father comes from Jacmel in Haiti. Naomi has one older sister called Mari, who is also a professional tennis player. Mari and Naomi were given their mother's maiden name because of Japanese law.

The law makes it mandatory for children with one foreign parent to be named after their Japanese parent. This enables them to be included in the Koseki (family registry).
