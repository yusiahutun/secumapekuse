Newcastle 'agree summer transfer for Alex Murphy' with 17-year-old set to join from Irish league

 Posted on March 27, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  217 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

NEWCASTLE UNITED have reportedly sealed a deal to sign highly-rated Irish youngster Alex Murphy.

The 17-year-old left-back has been chased by a host of European sides after impressing for Galway United 

That led to the likes of Celtic and Torino plotting a move for the Republic of Ireland youngster.

Both teams allegedly spoke to his representatives.

And Brighton were also interested in the defender.

But RTE Sport has claimed that Newcastle have won the race for his signature.

He is due to join the development squad at St. James’ Park. 

Murphy moved to his current club from Corrib Celtic and went on to make his first-team debut aged 16.

He only penned his first pro contract in January after he had been named as a standby for Republic of Ireland’s Under-21s squad in October.
