Bradley Cooper rushes out of interview urgently after 'call about his daughter'

 Posted on January 26, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  421 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Bradley Cooper had to hang up his actor shoes for a brief time while at a press conference after he was summoned to take care of his daughter.

The celebrity was in New York yesterday (December 21) for a press conference about his new film Maestro. He missed a call from his daughter’s school and asked if it “was all right” for him to step out and call them back. Bradley, who’s the director of the upcoming romance musical, shares his six-year-old with his ex-girlfriend, Irina Shayk.

Bradley Cooper rushes to be with his daughter

The event for the movie had only started 20 minutes prior when the 48-year-old checked his phone and saw he had a call from the youngster’s school. He asked if it was okay for him to leave and explained the reason for his abrupt absence to the audience.

Brad said to the crowd: “So sorry. The school nurse just called me. Can I just leave the room for a second to call them back and you can keep going. Is that all right?”

He continued by saying Lea’s school was a “10-minute walk” from the building in which the press conference was being held.

The A Star is Born actor added: “I have to go to the school to do something with Lea that needs… I have to apply something that they won’t allow… I have to do it, so it’s like a 10-minute walk.”

Bradley Cooper shares custody of his daughter with Irina, who he dated from 2015 until they split in 2019. Their daughter was born in March 2017.

Bradley hopes to ‘learn’ from his mistakes

Bradley Cooper has spoken about his daughter a few times over the years, but most recently told TV presenter Bear Grylls he “hopes” Lea will “learn from” his mistakes.

“I mean you learn from your predecessor’s mistakes and I’ll make tons that hopefully Lea will learn from and then being rigorous with myself to grow. To help unburden her with any of my bulls***,” said the father-of-one

After being asked if it was “one of the best things” the movie star could do, he responded: “And for me. It’s for me too. It just makes life much better.

He also said his little girl and becoming a parent was “a miracle and something I’ve always dreamt of.”
