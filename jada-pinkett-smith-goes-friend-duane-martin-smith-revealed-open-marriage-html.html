Jada's outing comes not long after Will, 53, recently revealed he has been with other women during his 14-year marriage, offering insight into his side of the non-monogamous relationship over a year after Jada's extramarital tryst with August Alsina came to light.

'Jada never believed in conventional marriage... Jada had family members that had an unconventional relationship,' the Men In Black actor said in the interview published Monday. 'So she grew up in a way that was very different than how I grew up.'

'There were significant endless discussions about, what is relational perfection? What is the perfect way to interact as a couple?' he went on. 'And for the large part of our relationship, monogamy was what we chose, not thinking of monogamy as the only relational perfection.'

He went on to say: 'I don't suggest our road for anybody. I don't suggest this road for anybody. But the experiences that the freedoms that we've given one another and the unconditional support, to me, is the highest definition of love.'

Candid: Will admitted to having an 'unconventional' marriage with wife Jada in a raw interview with GQ , out ahead of the release of his memoir Will in November 

Last year, Will and Jada candidly discussed the actress' 'entanglement' with singer August Alsina during an episode of her show Red Table Talk.

There, Jada said she developed a friendship with August four years ago, but revealed she and Will were going through a 'difficult time' and had 'basically broken up'. 

Now, the Aladdin star admits he was also having extramarital relationships, however, he refrained from going into detail about his extracurricular activities in the interview.

The Fresh Prince star did, however, discuss a previous fantasy he had, of having a harem of famous girlfriends.

He spoke about the idea with an intimacy coach named Michaela Boehm, and even had her work on the list with him and planned to contact the women, but ultimately realized the idea was 'horrific'.

The list included Halle Berry and ballerina Misty Copeland.  

He said: 'I don't know where I saw it or some s*** as a teenager, but the idea of travelling with 20 women that I loved and took care of and all of that, it seemed like a really great idea.

Family: The couple are seen with their children Trey Smith, Willow Smith, and Jaden Smith at an event in 2016

'And then, after we played it out a little bit, I was like, "That would be horrific. That would be horrific. I was like, "Can you imagine how miserable?"'

However, Will still found it helpful to discuss his fantasy in depth.

He explained to the men's lifestyle publication: 'What she was doing was essentially cleaning out my mind, letting it know it was OK to be me and be who I was.

'It was OK to think Halle is fine. It doesn't make me a bad person that I'm married and I think Halle is beautiful.

'Whereas in my mind, in my Christian upbringing, even my thoughts were sins. That was really the process that Michaela worked me through to let me realize that my thoughts were not sins and even acting on an impure thought didn’t make me a piece of s***.” 

Will and Jada share two children: daughter Willow, 20, and son Jaden, 23. Will has son Trey, 28, from his first marriage to Sheree Zampino.
