Sushi &amp; Sake Pairing Menu at all Sen-Ryo Outlets

 Posted on August 6, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  97 words  |  Martina Birk

Sen-Ryo Sushi is another Maxim’s chain. It’s not expensive and yet you often get delish and juicy sushi straight off the conveyor belt—so long as you don’t mind the wait at rush hours. Sen-Ryo has cooperated with Sake Culture Exchange Association to come up with a sake-pairing menu. Try pairing the whole horse mackerel (aji) sashimi ($120) with a bottle of Sakagura no Uta Jinjo ($145/300ml, $315/720ml). Horse mackerel has a tender texture and uniquely rich flavor, so a crisp and light-bodied sake works perfectly well with it. The menu is available in all Sen-Ryo outlets.
