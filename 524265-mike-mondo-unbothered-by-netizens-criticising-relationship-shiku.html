Mike Mondo Unbothered by Netizens Criticising His Relationship with Shiko

 Posted on June 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  470 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • Mike Mondo and his girlfriend Shiko Nguru have been trending online after they went public with their relationship
  • Netizens expressed mixed reactions, with some asking why the radio personality went for a woman with kids
  • This was days after Shiko's ex went on different podcasts to explain what led to his failed marriage

Radio host Mike Mondo has been trending online after it emerged that he was dating content creator Rama's ex and baby mama Shiko Nguru.

Who is Shiku Nguru's ex

Rama and Shiko became popular after launching their joint YouTube channel, Green Calabash, which was deleted when they parted ways.

While breaking up in 2022, Rama wrote on Instagram wishing his ex-wife Shiko all the best as she relocated to Nairobi and asked people not to pick sides concerning their split.

"Please don’t build a narrative around me because you feel the need to pick sides in a situation where no one is forcing you to pick sides. Two adults sat down and figured there is nowhere in the world people would sacrifice individual ambition and leave for emotional security as a couple," he said.

Mike Mondo defends relationship with Shiko

However, the mother of his kids is currently in a relationship with radio host Mike Mondo, and Kenyans online have been sharing mixed reactions about the relationship.

Some wondered why Mondo went for a woman with kids with another man, while others questioned why he kept posting his love life on social media.

But, the media personality took to social media to blast those with a problem with his relationship and told them to mind their business.

He said:

"At the end of the day, you who are complaining about Shiko and me continue. I am quite okay with all the love or hate you send my way. Usichokikula hakikufinyi, unye ama ule." (What you do not eat does not press you, whether you drink or eat).

The couple have been serving goals on social media despite Rama recently doing podcast tours where he shared insights about his failed marriage.

Mondo on being pressured to marry

The radio presenter once disclosed on air that his mum wanted him to bring someone home on his fortieth birthday.

The fine on-air personality shared that he kept getting pressure from people who reminded him that he was soon turning 40 but with no wife.

His fans empathised with him while some gave their own experiences dealing with the pressure that comes with turning 30 and 40.
