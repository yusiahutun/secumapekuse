Anne Kiguta Shows of All Grown Son with Jomo Gecaga During Movie Date: &quot;Prince Charming&quot;

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  394 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill
  • Anne Kiguta shared photos of her all-grown son with Jomo Gecaga after hiding him from social media for a long time
  • The former Citizen TV presenter went with her seven-year-old boy on a movie date, and she revealed they enjoyed every bit
  • Kiguta and Gecaga welcomed twins, a boy and a girl, in 2016 but separated and are co-parenting

Former TV presenter Anne Kiguta has, for the first time, shown off her all-grown son, Uhuru.

Who is Anne Kiguta's baby daddy?

Kiguta gave birth to twins, a boy, Uhuru, and a girl, Makenna, in July 2016. Kiguta's children were sired by former resident Uhuru Kenyatta's nephew, Jomo Gecaga.

The mother of three always keeps her children off social media, and he has never shown off the faces of the twins.

Kiguta and Gecaga parted ways, and during Father's Day, the father of five acknowledged all his offspring, warming hearts.

On June 18, Gecaga's third baby mama, Lola Hannigan, celebrated Jomo with a sweet message, describing him as an incredible dad. She shared an adorable video of her baby daddy strolling with their daughter, Malaika.

"Happy Father’s Day, my love. You are such an incredible dad," she captioned the video.

Commenting on Lola's post, Gecaga acknowledged all his five children, and he said:

"My Malaika, in name and in spirit. May you always watch over us and your siblings. @lolahannigan thank you, but it takes two. Udi, Uhuru, Makena, Malaika and X, thank you for entrusting me to be your dad ❤️."

Anne Kiguta spends quality time with son

The media personality posted photos from her movie date with her seven-year-old son on her Insta Stories, sharing their experience on the date.

"Prince charming and I had a super date mummy-son date as we were invited to watch the first ever film shot in space by HE, the Russian Amb to Kenya Dimitry Maksyimichev," she captioned a photo of them together.

Kiguta also posted a snippet of the movie, expressing how amazed she was with her son.

"My little guy could hardly blink as the rocker launched. Quite a feat by the Russian film crew. They beat Elon and Tom Cruise to this? Incredible," she said.
