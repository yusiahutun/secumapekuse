Songwriter And Producer Sam Merrifield Signs Publishing Deal With Position Music

 Posted on June 17, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  424 words  |  Aldo Pusey

L-R: Mike Torres, A&R, Joe Brooks, A&R, S. Merrifield, Mark Chipello, Head of A&R (Photo Courtesy: Delia Bush)

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – President and CEO Tyler Bacon announced that Sam Merrifield has signed a worldwide publishing deal with Position Music. The London-based songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist is a prominent collaborator in the pop and EDM genres and has recently worked with Lewis Capaldi, Calum Scott, NOTD, Daya, Two Friends, Ella Henderson, and more.

Merrifield has displayed his melodic and lyrical ability on hits like “American Psycho” by Marshmello, Mae Muller, and Trippie Redd, “Crying On The Dancefloor” by Sam Feldt, Jonas Blue, Endless Summer, and Violet Days, and UK Top 10 single “Good Without” by Mimi Webb. Merrifield has also had continued success in the singer-songwriter genre with “You & Me” by James TW, “closure” by Henry Moodie, and “We Were Young” by Petit Biscuit and JP Cooper. Recent collaborators include writers JKASH, Gregory ‘Aldae’ Hein, Ross Golan, Jack & Coke, Plested, Freedo, PHD, and Neil Ormandy. Merrifield has upcoming cuts with Marshmello, Cheat Codes, ILLENIUM, Mimi Webb, Gryffin, Zara Larsson, and more.

“From first meeting Mark, Joe, and Mike at Position six months ago, I could tell immediately that we had exactly the same vision for the next chapter,” says Merrifield. “Their hunger and passion for the music I create and get excited to make really gave me no doubt that we can achieve some great things together. I’m incredibly excited to see what the future holds!”

Merrifield is managed by Luke Williams of Closer Artists.

“Position’s multi-faceted A&R team felt like a great fit for Sam from the moment we met with Mark, Joe, and Mike. There is a genuine passion and hunger to succeed that is felt throughout the company. Sam’s a special talent who thrives across genres, and we wanted a partner that could accelerate his success across the board. Their culture and approach to publishing is exactly what Sam was looking for and we’re really excited to build with them,” says Williams.

“I first met and worked with Sam in my artist years,” says Joe Brooks, A&R at Position Music. “His talent and character left such an impression that when, some 7 years later, I heard he was looking for a publishing partner stateside, I jumped at the chance to be involved. He is one of the most consistent topliners I’ve known, always operating at the highest level and with impeccable professionalism. He is incredibly ambitious and I have no doubt we will have some massive songs together.”
