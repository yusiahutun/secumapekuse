Martha Stewart Wore the Denim Brand Hollywood Low-Key Wears on Repeat

 Posted on October 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  498 words  |  Larita Shotwell

There are some celebrities that I can always count on to deliver a 10/10 outfit, and Martha Stewart is one of them. Whether she’s wearing fall-perfect booties or a sweet denim dress, she clearly knows what she’s doing — and she continued her fashion streak last week.  

At a recent QVC event, Stewart wore a stylish Veronica Beard blazer with gold-toned buttons paired with comfy-looking Skechers sneakers. While those two components are standouts alone, I’d be doing you a disservice if I didn’t call out her iconic Rag & Bone jeans. Not only are they from a brand worn by a slew of stars and supermodels like Jennifer Aniston, Jennifer Lopez, Karlie Kloss, Katie Holmes and Kendall Jenner, but they’re perfectly chic, featuring a dark wash hue and slim silhouette.   

Shop Rag & Bone Jeans

The multi-hyphenate put her own spin on the celebrity-worn jeans by opting for a controversial skinny pair, which — call me crazy — may just be making a comeback. Kate Middleton has been wearing the style like clockwork, and Meghan Markle has been following close behind. While there’s been much debate over the last year on whether or not skinny jeans are officially out of style, they can definitely be practical when wearing knee-high boots or paired with slim heels for a sleek look.

But, the Stewart-worn brand isn’t just home to skinny jeans. The label has tons of denim offerings such as bootcut jeans, boyfriend fits, and high-waisted picks. So, shop the denim brand below, and snag your favorite celeb-approved option before someone else does.  

Featherweight Logan Wide-leg Jeans

These wide-leg Logan jeans may just be my favorite Rag & Bone style I’ve laid eyes on. Made of a lightweight denim, these non-stretch bottoms offer a full-length inseam with a spacious fit throughout the hips and thighs. The vintage-like inseams and pockets deliver the right amount of flair, while the worn-in look offers a casual vibe. Wear these with a tucked-in tee and your favorite loafers. 

Harlow High-Waist Fray-Curve Hem Jeans

If you’re looking for a practical but stylish pair of jeans, then consider the Harlow silhouette. Delivering a high-waistline, these babies include a fitted leg with an open, frayed hemline. The hem is also curved, taking the pants to another level. The dark wash elevates the overall look, so consider pairing these with sophisticated mules and a bodysuit. 

Featherweight Cailyn Cargo Jeans

The celebrity-worn brand also adheres to modern-day trends with its Cailyn Cargo jeans. The roomy bottoms fit right into the closets of Sofía Vergara, Taylor Swift, and Oprah, who are all fans of the utility style. Look forward to prominent stitching, a dark wash, and pocket overload. Style the cargo jeans with chunky dad sneakers and a baby tee for a Gen-Z approved look.
