During this period and era of digital innovation, almost all operations are performed on the web. It is even more convenient with the mobile apps. This makes it easier for users to access important information at any time and any place. The era of waiting for a long time for offices to open or having to beg officers for help is long gone, thanks to the Egerton Uni-Plus student portal. Scholars can now be served in a more effective and efficient way than before. This platform helps learners get the basic services that are offered at the university.

Egerton University is one of the oldest tertiary institutions in Kenya. It was started as a farm school in the early 90s. It was later upgraded to an Agricultural College teaching diploma programme. The Egerton Agricultural College Regulation was passed in 1955. Egerton University has created a portal to help learners to open an account and login to perform various activities that are academic related. The Egerton Uni-Plus site helps students have an easy time navigating from one option to the other. Scholars can easily access the university fee structure, exam results; register the units to be undertaken, booking Egerton University hostels, as well as following up the processing of students ID. A student is only required to fill in their student registration numbers (user name and password). Learners can change their passwords after logging in to their account.

Which services are available on the student’s portal?

Scholars access the main portal through the Egerton Student Portal. Scholars who wish to undertake distance learning can get the e-learning portal through the same portal. There is also a different page for student’s online application and those following up on admission. Learners seeking admission can use the student portal for placement services. What are the steps for creating a student account on Egerton Uni-Plus portal?

Below are the steps for Egerton University student portal registration.

  • Open the Egerton Uni-Plus portal.
  • Click on the “Student registration portal” link.
  • Click on “create a new account” link.
  • Fill in your details, that is, your first and last name, place of birth in the provided fields.
  • Then click next.
  • The email you used in the system will be displayed as your student email.
  • The system will automatically generate your username.
  • Fill in and confirm your password then click the submit button.

    • How do I register a course using the student portal?

    For course registration, follow the guideline below.

  • Visit the Egerton Uni-Plus student portal
  • Click on “student portal” link
  • Enter your username and password.
  • Then click on Academics, online registration.
  • Then proceed to, “Begin online registration”.
  • To search for the course to be registered, use the “Quick add functionality”
  • Type the course to be registered and then click “Add”.
  • When the course has been added, confirms it validity, then click “proceed”.
  • Finally click “register” to finish the process.

    Please have in mind that to get all the services named above, you must be a scholar at Egerton University. You also need a registration and reference number because the portal will ask for those details during registration. Just like all other websites, you might not remember all the important details. When this happens, you will need to reset it in order to get to the site. Use the same link to reset your account and click on “forgot password”. Then type in your registration number and the verification code. The code is not case sensitive. Complete this by submitting your request.
