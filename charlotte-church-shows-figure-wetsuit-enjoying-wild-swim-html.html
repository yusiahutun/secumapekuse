Charlotte Church revealed she has recently taken up a new hobby in the form of 'wild swimming' - as she shared snaps of the swim with her Instagram followers.

The singer, 35, posted a series of snaps of the dip on her social media, including one of her wearing a skintight wet suit while posing for a mirror selfie at home.

The candid snap gave a glimpse at Charlotte's stylish £2.5million Welsh home, where she recently gave her followers a tour of her stunning garden and grounds.

It comes after Charlotte, who secretly welcomed her baby daughter in August last year, shared a rare glimpse of her little one in an Instagram video last Wednesday.

She lives in the well-heeled village of Dinas Powys with her husband Jonathan Powell and their baby daughter, as well as her children, Ruby, 13, and son Dexter, 12, who she shares with her ex-partner, former Wales rugby player Gavin Henson.

In a cute video clip, Charlotte checked on the progress of her fruit trees, revealing she was growing blueberries, pomegranates and strawberries, before panning the camera over to her 'beautiful big blueberry', her baby daughter.

Charlotte then stepped up to the luscious green lawn where a little egret - a small, white heron - could be seen strutting around amongst the greenery. 

She completed the clip by panning around the vast open space, capturing sight of the school she hosts in the annexe.

The Welsh beauty recently opened her doors to local children, after setting up a private school in 2019 with a 20-person classroom where she hopes to 'liberate' children.

The singer, her husband and several teachers, welcomed 18 kids, aged 9-12 into the school last autumn after announcing she did not think the current school system works. 

The idea is part of Charlotte's The Awen Project - which she hopes will eventually grow into a charity which can set up other independent schools.
