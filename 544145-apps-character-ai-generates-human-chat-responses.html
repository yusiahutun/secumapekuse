1. ChatGPT

ChatGPT was created by OpenAI and launched in February 2023. It is the best alternative to Character AI and can answer questions and do tasks such as composing emails, code, essays, etc.

ChatGPT is one of the most used free apps like Character AI. However, the "ChatGPT Plus" subscription option costs $20 monthly. It has extra features such as faster response times, general access even at capacity, and internet access through plugins.

2. Charstar AI

If you’re looking for responses to your questions or tricky ideas, Charstar AI is the go-to chatterbox. With the official mobile app, you can create realistic AI friends, characters, and companions inspired by your favourite anime, stories, books, fantasies, TV shows or movies.

3. Chai AI

Chai AI has existed since 2021 but became famous in 2022. It's among the best apps like Character AI without filters.

The Chai app is free, but there's a message limit for unsubscribed users. Chai Premium costs $13.99 monthly or $134.99 yearly. Additionally, there's an ultra-subscription priced at $29.99 monthly or $269.99 yearly.

4. Janitor AI

Janitor AI is an advanced chatbot that uses natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to enhance data communication, automate tasks, and more.

It's a highly versatile platform that can understand and respond to users' queries while also doing the following: formatting data, cleaning data, framing column names, cross-tabulating data, etc.

5. Candy.AI

Candy.AI is a chat application that offers human-like dialogue and interactions for various purposes, including chatting via voice messages, deep conversations, emotional support and social interaction. Candy.AI is a freemium tool, but in its free usage, your messages are limited. However, its paid plan costs $9.99 monthly.

6. SpicyChat.AI

SpicyChat.AI, a web-based chatbot, pushes the boundaries of mature entertainment. It provides a virtual space that allows users to engage in open and exciting conversations without censorship, giving life to the wildest fantasies. With SpicyChat.AI, users can create virtual AI characters with personalities, names, and more.

7. Botify AI

Botify AI is a revolutionary platform that lets you chat with AI-powered characters, from real people to fictional characters. You can create your digital human and personalise every aspect, including mood, voice, biography and appearance.

8. Replika

The Replika app is an AI-based chatbot that mimics human conversations. It is advertised as an "AI companion" and generates natural-sounding responses to user queries. The Replika chatbot is intended to act as an AI sibling, friend or assistant. Some people use it to brainstorm, remember tasks, discuss their interests and more.

9. Tavern AI

Tavern AI is one of the best apps like Character AI without login. By employing its sophisticated algorithms and AI chat filters, users can easily craft character dialogues that blend harmoniously into narratives.

Tavern AI is free to use but relies on external APIs to generate text, and these APIs may charge for usage. For example, the OpenAI GPT-3.5 API charges $0.006 per 1,000 tokens generated.

10. Kajiwoto

Kajiwoto is one of the top apps like Character AI without login. It can do several things, including managing prompts, writing datasets and creating an AI voice. It's a free-to-use platform, but there's also a premium version that offers extra features. It costs $7.99 monthly or $87.89 yearly.

Is Charstar better than Character AI?

Both platforms offer similar features, but Charstar AI stands out with its ability to engage users in more natural and fluid conversations. Character AI has a more established user base and has been around for a long time.
