Nathalie Emmanuel looks stylish as she heads out in Notting Hill with beau Alex Lanipekun

Nathalie Emmanuel and boyfriend Alex Lanipekun co-ordinated their outfits as they headed out for a stroll in Notting Hill on Friday.

The couple, who have been together since 2018,  appeared to be in the autumnal mood as they both stepped out in brown tan jackets.

The Game Of Thrones actress, 32, looked lovely in a textured short sleeved cardigan, while Alex, 40, wore a casual jacket.

Showing off her nose piercing, Nathalie accessorised her outfit with a cream clutch bag and gold earrings and appeared to have stopped off at a store during their outing. 

Alex, meanwhile, kept things simple in a white T-shirt and green chinos.

Back in July, Nathalie revealed, despite her acting successes, she struggled with negative self-talk about her body image until social media changed her perception of herself. 

The actress explained how the memories function on social media made her realise she has been critical of her body for years and now looks back thinking: 'I look so great'.

She told Women's Health: 'You know how on Facebook it shows you a memory of yourself from six years ago or four years ago? A picture came up on my phone, and I went, "I look so great!"  

'But I also remember that at the time I thought I was overweight and needed to do all these unhealthy things. We just beat ourselves up constantly and we’re never perfect enough.'

After realising what she was doing, the Maze Runner actress said she had to change how she interacted with exercise and decided what she wanted to get from it.  

Nathalie added she has a particular passion for yoga, explaining she loves that there is 'no pressure to be the best at it'.

She explained: 'Even if you just lie on your mat for the hour and breathe with intention, you’re doing yoga.'
