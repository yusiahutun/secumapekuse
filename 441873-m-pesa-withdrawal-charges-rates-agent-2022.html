M-Pesa withdrawals

For M-Pesa withdrawals, you need to visit the agent shop near you and tap on the Withdraw Cash option on the SIM toolkit. Then, you should enter the amount and input your pin. Withdrawal charges for M-Pesa

Do you wish to withdraw money from an M-Pesa agent? Withdrawal of funds from an M-Pesa agent is not free. M-Pesa rates range between Kshs. 10 and Kshs. 300 depending on the amount of money you are withdrawing. The new withdrawal charges took effect on January 1, 2021, and they have remained unchanged in 2022. Here is a breakdown of withdrawal charges and rates.

Withdrawal of any amount below Kshs. 50 is free of charge. You will only be deducted Kshs. 10 for withdrawing any amount between Kshs. 50 and Kshs. 100. If you withdraw any amount between Kshs. 101 and Kshs. 500, you will be deducted Kshs. 27.

You will be deducted Kshs. 28 if you withdraw any amount between Kshs. 501 and Kshs. 2500. The transaction charges gradually increase up to Kshs. 300 when you withdraw any amount between Kshs. 50,001 and Kshs. 150,000.

Are M-Pesa withdrawal charges permanent?

It is imperative to note that the charges for M-Pesa withdrawal are not permanent, and they can be revised at any time. Ensure that you confirm the transaction rates on the list displayed in the agent's shop.

You can also query the transaction rates using the USSD code *234#. Then, select My M-Pesa info, choose the Tariff Query option from the list, and follow the prompts to know the rates for a given amount.
