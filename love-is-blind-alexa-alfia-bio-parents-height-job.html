Love Is Blind 3: Are Alexa Alfia And Brennon Lemieux Still Together?

Yes, Alexa Alfia and Brennon Lemieux are probably together.

From the moment the two met in the pods, there were sparks and a feeling of comfort in the air. Sharing their mutual passion for food, their perspective of families/heritage, and a bunch of other similarities, the two quickly connected.

No wonder, on the very first day, Alexa went as far as to say, “We’re the perfect match over here. Do you want to get married now?” To which, Brennon replied “I’m into it,” followed by a chuckle.

But it’s not always rainbows and butterflies. As days passed, the two opened up about the pain they both felt while growing up in broken homes. They talked about future expectations ranging from possible struggles to the importance of family approval to children, only to pull them close to each other.

So, after covering everything they wanted from their partner, Brennon said, “I love you” and Alexa reciprocated the same.

“I was extremely skeptical of this entire experiment. I guess maybe I’m just trying to figure out what being in love really is. But, I feel good. Alexa is making me a believer in this process. [She] makes me feel good; she makes me feel heard. She makes me feel comfortable, [and] trusted,” he shared at the time.

And the next minute, Brennon was down on his knee.

Despite the feeling that love was the only significantly scary thing missing from his life,  Brennon asked her to be his wife for he was obsessed with her.

Later, on their trip to Malibu, the two communicate more on romantic dates. And it seemed as if their sense of humor, personalities, as well as physical and emotional desires were the same.

Who Are Alexa Alfia Parents?

Alexa Alfia doesn’t have a good relationship with her divorced parents. At the age of 13, the star completely cut her mom off, and all the built-up anger and bitterness pushed her to leave home for Isreal to her grandparents. And it was only there, she felt what parental love was.

Now, Alexa’s dad, Adam Alfia is married to Morgan Alfia (Alexa’s stepmother). The last we checked, they lived in Dallas, Texas, where Adam worked as a Partner at Hot Chicks Nashville Hot Chicken, Playground Bar Uptown, and CutiePies Pizza. Besides this, Adam is also the owner of  Feedback,  Neon Cycle + Strength, and Theory Nightclub Uptown.

As for Alexa’s stepmom, Morgan married Adam on June 28, 2015. She also was the co-owner of  Neon Cycle + Strength.

Talking about Alexa’s siblings, she has 6 in total — an older sister Arielle Alfia, two younger brothers, a step-sibling, and a younger sister named Emma.

Her sister, Arielle is a Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) at Brookdale Northwest Hills. She was also an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant at The University of Texas at Austin.

More of Alexa’s family includes — Alon and Cara (cousins), Kfir Alfia (uncle),  Donna Alfia (aunt), and Barry Alfia (grandfather).

Sadly, Alexa’s grandmother,  Ziva Alfia, her “closest thing to a mom,” took her last breath in July 2020.

Recalling the time of her death, Alexa shared that they rushed to get to Israel in time for the funeral. Sadly, due to corona regulations, only a few of the family was there to say goodbye.

“There’s going to be a million moments throughout my life when I’m going to hear some top-notch gossip or want to wish you a Shabbat shalom or just want to hear your voice and I won’t be able to call you. Who am I supposed to tell all my boy problems to?” Alexa lamented.
