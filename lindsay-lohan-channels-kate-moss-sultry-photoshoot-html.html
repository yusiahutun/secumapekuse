After her photographic tribute to heroine Marilyn Monroe, Lindsay Lohan has moved on to a more modern day icon.

In an 18-page spread for Italian magazine Muse, a semi-nude Lindsay does her best to impersonates a grungy looking Moss in a steamy tribute to the supermodel's four-year relationship with Johnny Depp in the mid to late 90s.

Icon: Lindsay Lohan channelled model Kate Moss for a steamy photoshoot which took inspiration from the model's nineties relationship with actor Johnny Depp

Holed up: Lindsay and model Petey Wright portrayed the iconic couple in the semi-nude shoot at Hollywood's Chateau Marmont hotel

Three's company: The actress and model were later joined by dance Sofia Boutella who has worked with the equally outrageous Madonna

Shot at West Hollywood celebrity haunt Chateau Marmont, the photos show the 23-year-old troubled startlet in bed with male model Petey Wright in the role of a louche Depp. 

And dancer Sofia Boutella joins the duo for a photographic ménage a trois.

Other shots show Lohan, who's recently  been linked with actor Jason Segel, after splitting from girlfriend Samantha Ronson, flashing a breast and romps naked in bed with Wright.

Steamy: Lindsay wore a series of provocative outfits including a black lace bodystocking

Lindsay appeared to have run out of clothing as she emerged from the bedroom

Being Kate Moss: Troubled Lindsay enjoys being someone other than herself for the shoot

Photographer Yu Tsai said Lohan 'was totally comfortable with the nudity as long as it had artistic integrity.
