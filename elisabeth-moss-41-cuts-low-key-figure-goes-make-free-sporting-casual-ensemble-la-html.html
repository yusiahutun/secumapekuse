Elisabeth Moss cut a low-key figure as she stepped out in LA on Thursday.

The Handmaid's Tale actress, 41, was seen wearing a white T-shirt adorned with the words 'Los Angeles' alongside a pair of baggy black sweatpants and a pair of Adidas trainers as she took a break during a shoot.

The star looked fresh-faced as she went make-up free while making her way to her trailer with a Thermos cup and her phone in hand. 

Her blonde hair was worn loose and straight for the outing.  

The Mad Men actress has not been pictured publicly since May 2023, during which she was in England filming the FX mini-series The Veil.

The esteemed actress is very private when it comes to her love life, having only gone public with two relationships in the past.

She was previously married to comedian Fred Armisen, 57, - after which she described the marriage to Vulture as a 'traumatic' and 'horrible' experience. She was 26 and he was 42 when they tied the knot. 

She met the Saturday Night Live star in 2008 while her Mad Men co-star Jon Hamm was hosting the sketch comedy show.

They wed in October 2009 but separated eight months later. 

Elisabeth officially filed for divorce in September 2010 citing irreconcilable differences, before the divorce was legally finalized in May 2011.

In 2012, Elisabeth moved on with Australian cinematographer Adam Arkapaw after meeting on the New Zealand set of the mini-series Top of the Lake.

The relationship was confirmed by DailyMail.com at the time. The couple dated for three years before splitting in 2015. 

Over the years, she has been spotted on dates with 'mystery men' but she's yet to go public with another serious relationship.
