Karlie Kloss just cut her hair into a short bob

 Posted on August 20, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  220 words  |  Martina Birk

Karlie Kloss just unveiled a chic new chin-sweeping bob, joining an increasing list of A-list celebrities who have cut their hair short this autumn.

While the industry was distracted watching the Paris Fashion Week shows, the model-turned-entrepreneur popped to the hairdresser Jacob Rozenberg for the gorgeous cut which all but proves that bobs are back this season.

Dubbed the "blunt cut bob" by Rozenberg, who is an assistant to Kloss' regular hairdresser, celebrity hairstylist Harry Josh, the style is only slightly shorter than the wavy 'lob' that Kloss had recently been seen sporting on the front row.

That said, the cut is proof that getting rid of a few stray inches of hair can make all the difference, as the style perfectly frames Kloss' camera-worthy bone structure, while a centre-parting gives her extra volume at the roots without the need for waves.

If you needed any more of a reason to allow your hairstylist to pick up the scissors, social media has also been filled with overwhelmingly positive comments, with Irina Shayk, Kaia Gerber, the mum-to-be Ashley Graham and even Serena Williams sharing their love for the style.
