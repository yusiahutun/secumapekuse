  • Tadpole Athletics Test

    • Group: Side Quests

    Category: Pioneer Scouts

    Run through each of the checkpoints and reach the finish line on time

    This quest (actually, these are three similar, but separate quests) can be done in three different locations:

    Camp Venture: The starting point is located outside the camp, a dozen or so meters south of one of its gates.

    New River Gorge Bridge - East: The starting point is at the east end of the bridge.

    Sunnytop Ski Lanes: The starting point is located slightly to the north of the main building.

    Related points of interest

    Camp Venture Appalachia / West Virginia

    New River Gorge Bridge - East Appalachia / West Virginia

    Sunnytop Ski Lanes Appalachia / West Virginia

    Quest stages of Tadpole Athletics Test

    1. Listen to the instructions

    2. Complete the running test in time (0-100%)

    I've started the Athletics test at the Sunny Top Ski Lanes. I need to run through all the checkpoints before time runs out. Completing this will help me make progress toward my Athletics badge.

    Run along the route marked by yellow flags. You cannot leave any of the markers, as if this happens, the progress counter will stop.

    Camp Venture - Time limit: 1:00

    New River Gorge Bridge - East - Time limit: 3:15. The route goes to the other side of the bridge. You have to be careful not to fall down.

    Sunnytop Ski Lanes - Time limit: 3:15. The route runs down the hill, and is full of traps.

    Run to the first checkpoint / next checkpoints / finish line.
