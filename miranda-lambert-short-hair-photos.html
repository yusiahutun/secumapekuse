Miranda Lambert Shows Off New Short 'Do -- Like the Look?

 Posted on July 11, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  331 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Miranda Lambert is sporting a new 'do!

The country cutie took to Instagram on Monday to debut her short, layered tresses in a makeup-free photo with her hair stylist.

"Fresh cut! Fresh color! "Somethin' bout PLATINUM! Thanks @amandacraig12! Love you," she wrote along with the cute candid, referencing the title of her latest album.

Short hair seems to be the style of the summer -- Hilary Duff, Sienna Miller, Taylor Swift and Mandy Moore are also rocking cropped 'dos.

The 30-year-old singer kicked off her "Platinum" tour on August 8, and she's sounding better than ever. toofab got an exclusive look at her rockin' performance of "Little Red Wagon" -- check it out above!

Mrs. Blake Shelton says this single is among the most adventurous songs she's ever recorded.

"There's this girl named Audra Mae, and I kind of stalked her when I heard that song," Lambert explains in an interview with Billboard. "I was like, "I've got to talk to this girl, her record is amazing.' So we got to be friends, and we wrote a couple of times in L.A. She's from Oklahoma City and she lives in L.A., she's exactly my age, and we just started hanging out. Finally, one day I mustered up the courage and said, 'do you care if I cut Little Red Wagon, if I have the balls to do it, of course, because your version is so good?'"

"She said, 'sure, go ahead.' That's her singing on it, too. It's such a cool song," she continues. "I love attitude songs, but they can get old if you don't do 'em right. I don't want to keep doing the same type of attitude song, I've got to change them up. I can't wait to do this one live. It was so fun to cut and such a fun thing to do in the studio. It felt like me."

Gotta love Miranda!
