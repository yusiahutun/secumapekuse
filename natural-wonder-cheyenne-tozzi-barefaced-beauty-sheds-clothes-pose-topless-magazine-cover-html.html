Cheyenne Tozzi appears to pose topless for Gritty Pretty cover shoot

 Posted on October 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  588 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

She's the face of the inaugural summer issue of beauty magazine Gritty Pretty and model Cheyenne Tozzi perfectly fits the bill.

The 26-year-old Australian looks beautifully undone as she poses on the cover of the new title her hair salt sprayed, skin sun kissed.

The stripped back beauty appears to be sans clothes her limbs carefully concealing her modesty as she poses topless with barely a scrap of make-up covering her flawless features.

Inside the issue the multiple pages dedicated to the Sydney-born model reveal she's on the cusp of releasing her first solo single Reach You, which will be followed up by a 2015 album launch.

The globe trotting phenomenon has plenty of irons in the fire juggling modeling with music and TV commitments for reality shows such as The Face and now, Australia's Next Top Model.

Surf chick: The 26-year-old looks beautifully windswept in the shoot 

Stripped back: The Cronulla born beauty revealed she loves returning home to bathe in the ocean 

Pared back: The New York based model has lived in the States for eight years, but also calls Sydney and Barcelona her homes

But despite her passion for music the U.S-based model says: 'I just want to be able to say to my future kids, 'Look Mummy made an album!'

While she's lived in New York for eight years, running with the Australian IT pack that includes Cat McNeil, Lara Bingle and Shanina Shaik, the model says Sydney is where her heart is.

She loves nothing more than enjoying the waters near her Sutherland Shire home.

'If it's high tide in the morning I'll just go get in the bay and I take a body scrub (Frank Coffee Exfoliant, specifically) with me, too.'

Famous face: The IT girl wore a hint of mascara and lip gloss on her famed features 

Beach babe: The shoot celebrated the launch of Eleanor Pendleton's new online beauty magazine Gritty Pretty

Showing her support: Cheyenne is close friends with Eleanor Pendleton (pictured) who is the creative force behind the new magazine launch

Vision in white: The U.S based beauty splits her time between America, Australia and Barcelona

It's the simple things like being back in her old neighbourhood that she loves.  

'This isn't a frou frou town. You can walk barefoot up the road for a juice. My grandparents live above the fruit shop.'

The fashionista's star will gain even more traction in 2015 when she becomes a mentor on the ninth series of Australia's Next Top Model, starring alongside Jennifer Hawkins, Didier Cohen and Alex Perry.

A slew of high profile guests such as original super Linda Evangelista, Kelly Osbourne, Jean Paul Gaultier will make the show must-see viewing.

Gritty Pretty shoot: Photography, Daniella Rech; hair, Anthony Nader; makeup, Charlie Kielty; nails, Melisa Giraldo and styling, Ilona Hamer.

Dream team: Cheyenne will join Alex Perry, Jennifer Hawkins, Tyra Banks and Didier Cohen on Australia's Next Top Model
