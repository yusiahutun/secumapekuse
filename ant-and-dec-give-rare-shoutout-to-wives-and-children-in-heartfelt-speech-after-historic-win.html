Ant and Dec give rare shoutout to wives and children in heartfelt speech after big win

 Posted on January 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  579 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

The presenting duo won big for the 22nd time at the National Television Awards

    Ant and Dec had reason to celebrate when they won Best Presenter at the National Television Awards for the 22nd time on Tuesday evening and they were sure to give their loved ones a mention in their speech.

    The presenting duo, who are famed for their work on big shows such as and more, took to the stage at London's O2 Arena after being announced as winners and claimed they were "more nervous than ever" during the special moment.

    Ant began by saying: "Thank you so much to the NTAs, we've got quite a long list of thank yous. All of the shows and all of the people we work with, we are the luckiest two men on telly. We thank you from the bottom of our hearts."

    Dec interjected: "Thank you to our fantastic management team at YMU for your continued support," before thanking both of their loved ones on their behalf: "Thank you to our lovely wives, our beautiful children." At this point, Ant waved to the crowd and said sweetly: "Hi guys," as the camera panned and showed Dec's wife, Ali Astall and Ant's wife, Anne-Marie Corbett.
