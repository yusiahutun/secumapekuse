Burger King launches 12 days of Christmas deals including Whoppers for just 1

 Posted on May 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  407 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Fast food restaurant Burger King will be launching their 12 days of Christmas deals from tomorrow (December 1st) and there are some mouthwatering deals to get your hands on.

Fast food fans will be able to take full advantage of the restaurants' fantastic offers over the next 12-days, and to kick things off nicely over the next five days - here's exactly what you can get:

  • Wednesday 1st December: Whopper® burger for £1 (saving £3.99)
  • Thursday 2nd December: Six succulent Chicken Fries and a portion of regular fries for £3 (saving £2.68)
  • Friday 3rd December: Buy one get one free regular fries for £1.79 (saving £2.79)
  • Saturday 4th December: Free portion of fries and drink with a Gourmet Kings Argentinian Burger for £6.99 (saving £4.48)
  • Sunday 5th December: Free portion of regular fries with Memphis BBQ King Burger for £6.29 (saving £2.29)

So on Wednesday, should you be in need of a late breakfast, lunch, afternoon snack or something succulent for dinner, you will be able to get your hands on a succulent delicious flame-grilled Whopper for just £1 – making a tasty total saving of £3.99. How good is that?

The offer is on until Sunday, December 12th and is only available via the Burger King App which you can download via the Apple Store and Google Play.

Don't panic if what you fancy isn't on the festive 12-day menu as there's still lots that you can order from the restaurant's main menu.

Burger fans can get their hands on a £3 'APPY HOUR which includes a Bacon Double XL, Roadhouse Crispy Chicken, or a Double Whopper. The deal is on offer 2pm to 5pm weekdays only, and you can redeem either in store or add to your mobile order ready for you to click & collect.

Customers can also treat themselves to a Triple Stacker that includes three hamburgers, onion rings and cheese galore and barbecue sauce for £3, a Double Stacker for £2, and a Single Stacker for £1, or a Bacon Double Duo meal that includes fries and a drink for just £10.99.

If you're still feeling festive and fancy a sweet treat then why not treat yourself to this amazing deal - Amazon slash £24 off the price of giant Toblerone bar for Black Friday? And if you're making a party of it with friends - Amazon is selling 2 bottles of spirits for £20 and the offer lasts until January.
