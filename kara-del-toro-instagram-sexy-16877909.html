The swimsuit model and Instagram sensation has built up quite the fanbase thanks to her mind-blowing social media snaps.

And it’s hardly surprising, just look at the woman!

The 25-year-old Texan – who is signed to Elite models - can regularly be found showcasing her incredible physique in what appears to be her favourite item of clothing – a skimpy bikini.

But, even those sexy two pieces can get a bit boring at times, as Kara Del Toro shows in her one of her many saucy snaps.

Taking to her official Instagram page, the bombshell beauty left her followers’ jaws on the floor, as she posed up a storm in nothing but a pair of tiny undies– and there really wasn’t a lot to them either.

Throwing a seductive pout at the camera, the bronzed goddess flashed every inch of her curves, and plenty of her ample cleavage too.

But, keeping it classy, Kara - who has worked for several big name brands, including GUESS – ensured she left something to the imagination (just about) thanks to some strategically placed hands.

After popping on a woven beach hat and a pair of simple gold earrings, the look was complete – although it's not a look we'd suggest for any public occasions.

She captioned the eye-boggling post: “Hi Mom.”

Needless to say, the stunning blonde was swiftly inundated with comments from her legion admiring Instagram followers.

On user wrote: “My jaw is literally on the floor right now.”

A second chimed: “Oh my God, you are absolutely gorgeous!”

While a third admirer asked: “It must be awkward when your parents see your photographs, no?”

A final ballsy commentator joked: “Don't be a tease, give me a high five.”

Taking a look at Kara’s Instagram, we’re going to go out on a limb and say that there will be plenty more where that came from.
