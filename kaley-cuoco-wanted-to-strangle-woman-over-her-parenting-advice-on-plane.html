Kaley Cuoco wanted to 'strangle' woman over her 'parenting advice' on plane

 Posted on May 29, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  526 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Kaley Cuoco welcomed her first daughter, Matilda, in March 2023, claiming motherhood has exposed her to “a lot of judgments”.

New mom Kaley Cuoco and partner Tom Pelphrey welcomed their first child, Matilda, in the spring of 2023. The couple are still getting acquainted with parenthood and embarked on their first plane flight as a family of three – and it didn’t go smoothly.

Kaley Cuoco recalls ‘unbelievable’ passenger request on flight with baby

Kaley, 38, has shared special moments about life as a new mom on Instagram. But she gave an unfiltered account of a Thanksgiving situation that left her “so angry” on Jimmy Kimmel Live.

During their first family plane flight in November, Kaley brought Matilda’s sound machine to encourage her to sleep. “We’re having one of those flights that you hear that other people have, and you’re like, ‘Oh, that sucks for them.’ It actually happened to us,” she noted.

The nine-month-old was restless but eventually fell asleep. Moments later, a flight attendant suggested they turn it off at the request of another passenger.

“I couldn’t believe she asked us to turn it off,” Cuoco exclaimed. “Can you believe that? We were so angry”.

The celebrity couple discovered it was the woman in front of them, who couldn’t resist giving a sarcastic comment. “Oh, so your daughter does know how to smile?” she slammed in response to Matilda’s now-cheerful mood.

“I could have strangled her,” the Big Bang Theory actress added.

Kaley Cuoco and Tom Pelphrey’s relationship timeline

April 2022

Kaley separated from her second husband, equestrian Karl Cook, in September 2021, seven months before meeting Ozark actor Tom Pelphrey. Her divorce was finalized in June 2022.

The stars met at the Ozark premiere in April after being set up by their manager. Kaley gushed that their meeting was “love at first sight”.

May 4, 2022

They went Instagram-official in May when Pelphrey posted Polaroid photos of the two of them together alongside a heartfelt caption.

May 23, 2022

Their first public appearance came at the end of May to support Flight Attendant executive producer Greg Berlanti’s Hollywood Walk Of Fame ceremony.

July 12, 2022

The couple who gets Emmy nominated together stays together. Both Kaley and Tom receive nominations and of course, they were each other’s biggest fans.

“CONGRATULATIONS TO MY FAVORITE ACTRESS!!!” he captioned an Instagram video. “Such beautiful, brave work. Beyond grateful to share this with you today…”

Cuoco was up for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Comedy Series for Flight Attendant, while Ozark received six nominations. Kaley was defeated by Hacks’ Jean Smart, but Julia Garner won Best Supporting Actress on behalf of Ozark.

September 12, 2022

Kaley Cuoco and Tom Pelphrey make their red carpet debut as a couple at the 2022 Emmy Awards 

October 11, 2022

Cuoco announced she was expecting her first baby on Insta with a series of sweet photos, while Pelphrey shared the joyful news in a separate post.

March 30, 2023

Matilda Carmine Richie Pelphrey incoming! The couple announced the arrival of their daughter, calling her a “little miracle.”

“Eternally grateful for the strength and bravery of my soulmate and best friend,” Tom wrote of the actress.
