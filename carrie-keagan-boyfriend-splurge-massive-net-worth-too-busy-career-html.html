When celebrities keep their personal life private, they are often linked with someone of their opposite sex. And that someone is either their colleagues or the person who is seldom spotted with them.

It’s true that not every celebrity would like us to peek into their personal life but the rumors regarding their love life often create confusion.  Yet it’s interesting to learn one’s personal life, isn’t it?

Well, our lucky contender for today is Carrie Kegan. Let’s find out whether she is involved in a romantic relationship or is too busy to get married because of her blooming career.

Has a boyfriend to splurge massive net worth: 

Carrie Keagan is often linked with many re-known celebrities but hasn’t commented on any of such rumors. In October 2013, Carrie, who earns the net worth of $3 million was reported to be dating Michael Sheen, the ex-husband of Kate Beckinsale. 

A source talked about VH1’s “Morning Buzz” host Carrie and her alleged boyfriend Masters of Sex star Michael’s relationship to Us Weekly where he said that the romance is new and Carrie really likes Michael.

Carrie now seems to be busy inclining her career to the summit of success.
