Thai Airways is the national carrier of Thailand.

Thai Airways, also known as “TG,” is the flagship carrier of Thailand, operating domestic and international flights to over 80 destinations worldwide.

Thai Airways was founded in 1960.

Established in 1960 as a joint venture between Thailand’s domestic carrier, Thai Airways Company, and Scandinavian Airlines System (SAS), Thai Airways has grown to become one of the leading airlines in Southeast Asia.

Thai Airways has a fleet of over 80 aircraft.

With a diverse fleet consisting of Airbus and Boeing aircraft, Thai Airways ensures a comfortable and safe travel experience for its passengers.

Thai Airways is a member of the Star Alliance.

As a member of the Star Alliance, the largest global airline alliance, Thai Airways offers its customers access to a vast network of destinations and seamless travel connections.

Thai Airways has won numerous awards.

Over the years, Thai Airways has been recognized for its excellence in service and commitment to customer satisfaction, receiving awards such as “Best Economy Class” and “Best Business Class” by various prestigious organizations.

Thai Airways offers a range of diverse inflight dining options.

Passengers flying with Thai Airways can enjoy a variety of delicious meals, inspired by both Thai and international cuisines, served with warm Thai hospitality.

Thai Airways prioritizes passenger comfort.

With spacious seating, in-flight entertainment systems, and attentive cabin crew, Thai Airways ensures that passengers have a pleasant and enjoyable journey.

Thai Airways provides a range of travel classes.

From economy to Royal First Class, Thai Airways offers different travel classes to cater to the diverse needs of its passengers, providing a personalized and luxurious travel experience.

Thai Airways has a renowned frequent flyer program.

The Royal Orchid Plus program rewards loyal Thai Airways passengers with exclusive benefits and privileges, including access to airport lounges, priority check-in, and extra baggage allowance.

Thai Airways plays an active role in corporate social responsibility.

Thai Airways is committed to sustainable practices, community development, and environmental conservation, actively contributing to the betterment of society.

Thai Airways has a strong focus on safety.

With a dedicated team of highly trained professionals and strict safety protocols, Thai Airways maintains a strong safety record, ensuring the well-being of its passengers.

Thai Airways offers a comprehensive cargo service.

In addition to passenger services, Thai Airways operates a reliable cargo service, facilitating the transportation of goods and products worldwide.

Thai Airways has a state-of-the-art maintenance facility.

Thai Airways has its own maintenance, repair, and overhaul (MRO) facility, equipped with advanced technology and staffed by skilled technicians, ensuring the highest standards of aircraft maintenance.

Thai Airways supports cultural promotion activities.

Thai Airways takes pride in promoting Thai culture around the world, sponsoring various cultural events and activities that showcase the richness of Thai heritage.

Thai Airways offers a seamless online booking experience.

With its user-friendly website and mobile app, Thai Airways provides passengers with a convenient and hassle-free booking process, allowing them to plan and manage their travel with ease.

FAQs

1. What is the history of Thai Airways?

Thai Airways was founded in 1960 and has been serving as the national carrier of Thailand ever since. It has a long-standing reputation for providing world-class service and has received numerous awards and accolades throughout its history.

2. What destinations does Thai Airways fly to?

Thai Airways operates flights to various destinations worldwide. It has a comprehensive network that covers major cities in Asia, Europe, Oceania, and the Americas. Whether you’re planning a trip to Bangkok or looking to explore other parts of the world, Thai Airways offers a wide range of options.

3. What amenities does Thai Airways offer onboard?

Thai Airways ensures a comfortable and enjoyable journey for its passengers by providing a range of amenities. This includes spacious seating, in-flight entertainment, delicious meals, and a selection of beverages. Passengers can also enjoy the convenience of onboard Wi-Fi on select flights.

4. How can I book a flight with Thai Airways?

You can easily book a flight with Thai Airways through their official website or through various online travel agencies. Simply enter your travel details, select your desired flight, and follow the booking process. You can also contact their customer service for assistance with your booking.

5. What is the baggage allowance on Thai Airways?

The baggage allowance on Thai Airways depends on the type of ticket and the destination. Generally, economy class passengers are allowed to bring one piece of carry-on luggage and check-in luggage within the weight and size limits specified by the airline. Business and first-class passengers have higher baggage allowances.

6. Does Thai Airways offer any loyalty programs?

Yes, Thai Airways offers a loyalty program called Royal Orchid Plus. Passengers can earn miles on eligible flights and redeem them for various benefits, including flight upgrades, lounge access, and additional baggage allowance. The program has multiple tiers, with higher tiers offering more exclusive privileges.

7. How can I contact Thai Airways for customer support?

You can reach out to Thai Airways customer support through their official website or by calling their contact center. They have dedicated customer service representatives who can assist you with any queries, booking changes, or other concerns you may have.

8. Does Thai Airways have any special services for passengers with specific needs?

Thai Airways is committed to ensuring a comfortable journey for all its passengers, including those with specific needs. They provide special assistance for passengers with disabilities, medical conditions, and other requirements. It is recommended to inform the airline in advance so they can make appropriate arrangements.
