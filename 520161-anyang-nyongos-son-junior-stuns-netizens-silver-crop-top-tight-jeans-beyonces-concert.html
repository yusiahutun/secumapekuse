Anyang' Nyong'o's Son Junior Stuns Netizens with Silver Crop Top, Tight Jeans at Beyonce's Concert

 Posted on October 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  412 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon
  • Governor Anyang' Nyong'o's son Junior was among the people who attended Beyonce's Renaissance sold-out tour
  • He dropped photos of his fine look that left social media users with mixed reactions as they critiqued his look
  • Junior rocked a light silver crop top and tight pants and spent the night taking photos with his wife, Wanja and friends

Kisumu Governor Anyang' Nyong'o's son, Junior Nyong'o, has been known to stretch beyond society's expectations and set new trends for himself.

The actor dropped fine photos of how he showed up for the famous Renaissance concert by American singer Beyonce.

Inside Junior Nyong'o's concert look

Junior donned a silver crop top, a dark silver tight pants and binocular glasses in the series of photos he shared.

He posed at home holding what looked like a pole and behind him a couch that matched his pants.

He also shared a video showing off his wife, Wanja, who rocked a short silver dress with spaghetti straps. She also covered it up with green sleeves.

Junior also shared more photos in the bathroom and another photo with his wife and friends.

"I pay them all in dust. Are we collectively ever going to recover from this Renaissance era?" he said on Instagram.

His photo left netizens with mixed reactions. Some, particularly in the US thought the look was elegant while Kenyans on the comment section asked him why he decided to go for the design.

Junior, wife set fashion trend at movie premier

In September 2022, junior and his siblings were among the notable people who graced the Black Panther movie premier held in Los Angeles.

He stepped out looking dapper in a white suit accompanied by his wife Wanja, who was in a long orange dress.

Netizens commended the two for looking like icons as they supported Lupita, who is the lead actress in the superhero film.
