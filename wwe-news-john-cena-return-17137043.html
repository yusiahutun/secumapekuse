WWE legend John Cena makes return to ring with new look sparking hair transplant rumours

 Posted on September 22, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  346 words  |  Larita Shotwell

WWE top star Cena has been taking time away from the ring due to Hollywood commitments, with the latest being a starring role in blockbuster Bumblebee.

But with Wrestlemania season round the corner and the company lacking stars, the multi-time world champion made his return to TV on Smackdown last night.

Owner Vince McMahon announced on an untelevised show on Boxing Day that Cena would be appearing on both brands in the future.

He teamed with megastar Becky Lynch to defeat the team of Andrade Almas and Zelina Vega.

Fans were quick to praise the legend who appeared not to have missed a step despite being away from the ring for a long time.

However, others were fascinated by his full head of hair.

Even WWE’s official Twitter account got in on the act, writing: “New hair, same John Cena.”

WWE fanatics were quick to mock the superstar, claiming he was going through a midlife crisis.

One wrote: “John Cena be looking like an evil businessman from an action move with his new hair.”

Another added: “Man John Cena looks so weird with a full head of hair.”

Others remarked of his resemblance to Homer Simpson, after he regrew his hair.

And this led to speculation Cena may have had a hair transplant.

Tim Burr wrote: “He's probably just growing enough length for coverage of a hair transplant donor area. “

Tiffany added: “I still wanna know if John Cena got a hair transplant tho. Cuz he was thinning in 2010 and it's 2019 [sic].”

And another joked: “So cool of Phil Dunphy to donate his hair to John Cena.”

Cena previously explained he grew out his hair for a film role, and due to its reaction has no plans of cutting it.

He said: “I’ve been playing a character in WWE for 15 years now, and I’ve looked the exact same.

“Then I left to go to China for about six months and I came back with this, and literally, it has caused a title wave of emotional upheaval. I’ve literally ruined people’s childhoods.”
