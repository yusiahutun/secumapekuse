Anne Hathaway nails courtside style in an edgy all-black outfit

 Posted on March 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  274 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

This weekend, Anne Hathaway was spotted enjoying some basketball at Atlanta’s State Farm Arena, and — as with all her fashion choices — her courtside style did not disappoint.

It was an action-packed game between the Atlanta Hawks and Charlotte Hornets, with the home team clinching the win 132-91. However, even so, our eyes were glued to the stands at Hathaway's ultra-cool all-black ensemble.

For the occasion, the actress kept things casual in a classic inky-black ribbed tank top with wide straps and a matching zip-up hoodie that she wore open and down along her arms. She upped the edginess and the ante with a chunky silver chain necklace, small silver hoops, and a pair of oversize black-frame shades with purple-tinted lenses. She kept her make-up minimal, fresh, and rosy and wore her chestnut locks down and straight with her wispy bangs across her forehead.

Rick Kern//Getty Images

It was a different look for Hathaway, who has been embracing a girly style of late. Earlier this month, at the world premiere of her new film The Idea of You, Hathaway wore a teeny-tiny opulent white mini dress that featured thick straps, a wide V neckline, rectangular gold studs arranged in a chevron pattern below the bust and along the hem, and small sequins that covered every inch of fabric. She finished off the glimmering look with strappy, open-toe platforms with a tall chunky heel, plus a diamond tennis necklace. She rocked another shiny ensemble days before at the 39th Film Independent Spirit Awards in a milky-white sequin- and crystal-embellished Valentino jumpsuit.
