Soap Awards 2017: Best red carpet looks

 Posted on October 2, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1090 words  |  Martina Birk

    ' Amanda Clapham dazzled in a vibrant red Suzanne Neville dress. The star showed off her stunning look on her Instagram page and revealed she had been styled by Kelly Hidge and had her make-up done by celebrity artist Benny Hancock.

    ' Anna Passey looked effortlessly elegant in a Malan Breton gown. The Best Actress nominee's hair was styled by "goddess" Jayne Littler from Andrew Collinge whilst her make-up was done by Thea at the Cassie Lomas Makeup Academy.

    Bhavna Limbachia, who plays Rana in looked stunning in a pink dress by Liverpool-based fashion designer Mark Melia. The star thanked the team behind her look on Instagram, she said: "A huge THANK YOU for making me feel like a million dollars last night at the Soap Awards".

    Former HELLO! Online blogger Catherine Tyldesley was the belle of the ball in a Mark Melia pink halter neck dress. The star paid tribute to the victims of the Manchester terror attack by accessorising her look with a clutch that had the words "we stand together" and a drawing of a bee, the symbol of the city, emblazoned on it.

    's Adam Thomas and fiancée Caroline Daly posed together on the red carpet. Adam looked dapper in a Whitfield & Ward suit and Caroline looked glam in a Lola Loves Boutique ensemble.

    Corrie's Brooke Vincent's ensemble was also a Mark Melia design. The star shared a close-up picture of her stunning make-up and captioned it: "ThankYou So Much @nealeyleah @sci_u @lovezeroskin @she_vip_manchester @salon_fifty_one For Helping Make Me Look Half Decent On Saturday ?"

    Bride-to-be Jacqueline Jossa might have been giving fans a peek into what her wedding dress might look like by wearing a stunning silver gown that featured gorgeous embellishments. The EastEnders star is set to marry fiancé Dan Osborne this summer.

    's Fiona Wade wore a bespoke white leather basque and full white maxi skirt by Manchester-based Turkish designer Zeynep Kartal.

    's Charley Webb and husband Matthew Wolfenden looked the picture of happiness as the posed for photographers ahead of the ceremony, which saw take home the gong for Best Soap.
