As a barrier against brisk cold weather, opt into the women’s coat trends for 2024. Use the outerwear heroes as a way to stay warm, a stand-alone statement, or an element to tie your look together. Whether you’re attending a formal dinner, weekend brunch with friends, a winter wedding, or simply out to run errands, these women’s coat trends bring all the fashion.

There are options that are bold, on-trend, classic, and weather-friendly. And even if you don’t live in a city that has distinct seasons, the below trend edit deserves a spot in your wardrobe. So what can you expect for 2024? You’ll see familiar trends like maxi coats and slouchy trench coats on the list, but also a couple of wild cards like scarf coats and duvet coats that are so cozy you might even wear them inside. 

And between animal print, florals, solid colors, or quiet-luxury inspired coats — there really is something for everyone. Plus, if you find inspiration in any of the six women’s coat trends, you’ll be able to shop them now with our expert edit.
