Three Times Kandi Burruss Dazzled in a Bikini

 Posted on May 13, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  721 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Kandi Burruss donning a bikini is as iconic as some of her famous songs. Here are three times the RHOA TV star’s look sent tongues wagging.

Since joining the franchise in 2009, “The Real Housewives of Atlanta” star Kandi Burruss has become the TV show’s mainstay. With Kandi set to return as a cast member for the new season, she’ll become the longest-running housewife on “RHOA.”

Besides being a part of the show’s main cast, Kandi is also a successful songwriter, producer, actress, and singer. Before her stint on reality TV, Kandi dominated the world of music. Nowadays, she is doing the same to reality TV due to her impact on “RHOA.”

However, TV Kandi has mastered the fashion world in addition to music and reality. Being cast on RHOA allowed her to show off her fashion talents, and Kandi didn’t disappoint. The beautiful star never shies away from showing off her body, donning beautiful outfits during each episode of “RHOA.”

Still, one outfit that seems to generate more attention towards Kandi’s banging body is a bikini. The beautiful star knows how to bend the two-piece to her will, and she oozes sexiness while donning a bikini. Below are the three times the TV personality slayed in a bikini.

Kandi’s High-Waist Bikini

Sometime in 2020, the songwriter, while discussing how she gained more weight amid the Covid19 lockdown, shared sexy snaps of herself at the beach with her followers on Instagram. The pictures showed a thicker Kandi in a blue two-piece bikini by Louis Vuitton.

The first snap captured the musician barefoot, standing on the beach and rocking large Le’ Archives star-shaped diamond earrings. The stylish earrings complimented the silver detailing on her bikini, which consisted of a halter bodice and a high-waisted bottom.

In the second slide, Kandi spiced things up a little by wearing a pair of silver stiletto heels. She then posed while backing the camera, and glancing over her shoulder. This time, she posed on the wooden steps that led to the sands on the beach. Despite adding extra Lbs, Kandi looked stunning, with her gorgeously toned body. The post sent her fans drooling in the comment section.

Kandi’s Slays in a Lemon Bikini 

During a significant cast shake-up for the RHOA’s show in July 2021, Kandi decided to check in on her fans on Instagram. So the TV star posted two sneak pics on her Instagram to remind her fans of her endearing figure.

In the first post, she styled a two-piece patterned lemon bikini with the top comprising spaghetti-freezy sleeves and a hipster-shaped bottom. The bottom featured a strap around the band, which was stylishly tied around her waist to give a perfect body figure. She, however, complemented the look with bright yellow sandal stilettoes.

However, the second snap captured the 46-year-old Kandi with her friends from work– Shamea Morton and Carmon Cambrice, in similar clothing under a shade tree with little sunlight reflecting their toned skin. Needless to say, the TV personalities flaunted their sexy summer bodies in a classy way, making fans want more of their goodness.

Kandi’s Bikini in Thailand Look

In 2019, shortly after the RHOA’s reunion show, where the host, Andy Cohen, and Kandi shared their thoughts on surrogacy- amid other dramas from co-stars, Kandi took a vacation with her husband to Phuket, Thailand. To keep her fans updated, the RHOA star littered her Instagram page with many lovely posts from the trip.

Some of those pictures featured her husband, Todd Tucker, while the couple chilled at a resort. In two of these posts, Kandi stunned in a paired bright yellow bikini. The bikini consisted of a top with a widely cut neckline displaying her cleavage and a bottom that hugged her hips. Meanwhile, Kandi rocked the bikini differently in the two posts.

In the first post, she styled it with a white and black buttonless jacket, and her priceless smile filled the picture. In the second post, she dazzled in the bikini top matched with a mini skirt made from the same fabric as the jacket.

This time, the singer sat pretty on a boat with her man, Todd, and they had some lovely and romantic moments. Still, Kandi’s jaw-dropping snaps triggered fans’ to appreciate their favorite TV star in the comment section with so much flattery, compliments, and praise.
