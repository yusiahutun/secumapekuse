The One Show turns awkward as Angelica Bell confronts Kylie Minogue over leaving the UK

 Posted on August 7, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  455 words  |  Aldo Pusey

A pin could have dropped in the studio for The One Show as host Angelica Bell quizzed Kylie Minogue over her decision to flee the UK.

The 53-year-old hitmaker was left a touch surprised as the presenter managed to squeeze in the question at the end of the interview - catching her off-guard.

Kylie thought she'd escaped questioning on her choice to head back to her home in Australia but Angelica wasn't letting her get away with it that easily.

Angelica said: "Kylie, I will make some time for this. Because you caused a Twitter storm, as you usually do.

"Saying that you are going to leave?"

Gasping Kylie said: "Ohhh, well I didn't say anything, they were like 'she's announced' I didn't.

"I'm getting texts from really good friends saying 'can I see you before you go?'

"I'm like 'babe, I gunna be here probably as much' it's just my base and my things will be in Australia, with my family.

"But yeah, in a months time you'll be saying 'she's still here.'"

Kylie appeared on the programme alongside Years & Years star Olly Alexander.

The pair were on the show together to promote their new hit single A Second To Midnight - providing a world exclusive performance at the end.

Talking about their union, Kylie and Olly explained that they had been "friends for years" before deciding to make music together.

Blushing, Olly said: "I mean, it's Kylie. Who can say no to Kylie, right?"

Hosts Ronan Keating and Angelica took questions from fans who had video'd in.

One person noticed that Kylie had been wearing a similar leopard print coat in a former music video and wondered if the star had re-worn the vintage item.

The chart topper explained that sadly it "wasn't the same coat" as she no longer had access to some of the clothes from back in the day.

Which lead to the Year & Years singer gushing about filming with the Australian-born songstress.

He beamed: "I actually think the day we filmed A Second To Midnight was the best day of my life."

A touch bashful, Kylie responded: "Awww."

The One Show airs weekdays at 7pm on BBC One.
