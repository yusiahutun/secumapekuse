According to reports from The Athletic, Mbappé is currently taking home one of the biggest salaries in football, setting PSG back €200 million (US$213 million) in 2024. The player, however, doesn’t seem to be driven entirely by money. Per Sky Sports, he is rumoured to be prepared to take a pay cut to join Real Madrid this summer.

Of course, such big pay cheques fuel a glamorous lifestyle and a love of luxury goods. In particular, the striker has a passion for luxury watches: he has been brand ambassador for Swiss luxury watchmaker Hublot since 2018, and has been spotted wearing many of its creations.

He can certainly afford the timepieces too, since it’s reported that he earns £1 million (US$1.2 million) a year from the brand, according to Sport Pro. His collection includes the gem-bezel Big Bang Unico Titanium Rainbow and Big Bang E Fifa World Cup Qatar 2022 smartwatch.

Here’s a peek into Mbappé’s luxury watch collection.

1. Hublot Big Bang Unico Red Sapphire

This automatic chronograph features a flashy red sapphire crystal, one of the more unusual and eye-catching materials that Hublot uses to forge its cases. Measuring 45mm, the transparent watch is fitted with a black rubber strap. The design also comes in a version with a ruby set bezel.

2. Hublot Spirit of Big Bang Titanium White Pave

Posing on Instagram alongside fellow watch lover Jay-Z, Mbappé shows off this all-white Hublot piece with a tonneau-shaped case. The piece has a self-winding skeletonised chronograph movement with a 50-hour power reserve. The titanium case is set entirely with diamonds and paired with a white rubber strap.

3. Hublot Spirit of Big Bang King Gold

It seems the football star is a huge fan of the Spirit of Big Bang aesthetic, owning a gold version of this timepiece, which features a chronograph movement similar to the Titanium White Pave number.

4. Hublot Big Bang e Gen3

Mbappé is among the first to wear this new watch, which is likely one of his latest acquisitions. The Uefa Euro 2024 rendition of this connected watch has been created especially for the event, and is a follow-up to the version that was launched for the 2022 Fifa World Cup.

The ceramic piece is powered by Google Wear OS 3 and is compatible with various apps available on Google Play. Mbappé wears a version with a pastel blue strap.

5. Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Offshore

Mbappé’s appetite for watches goes beyond Hublot. He is an avid collector and owns pieces from Audemars Piguet and Rolex, among other top brands. However, since his relationship with Hublot, he seems to have cast these other timepieces aside and now wears Hublots exclusively.

Before his relationship with Hublot, he was often spotted wearing an Audemars Piguet classics, the Royal Oak Offshore. That and the Royal Oak are among the most popular references from Audemars Piguet, and are favoured by celebrities, rappers and athletes alike.

Mbappé’s version is a chronograph and is paired with a classic Royal Oak Offshore rubber strap.
