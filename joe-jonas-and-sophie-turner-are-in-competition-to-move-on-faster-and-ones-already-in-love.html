Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner 'are in competition' to move on faster - and one's already in love

 Posted on March 8, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  463 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It looks like Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner have well and truly moved on from each other after their shock divorce towards the end of last year. According to one source, they may not have forgotten about each other so easily, as they claim they’re still in ‘competition’.

The celebrity couple shocked the world when they announced their divorce in September, after two children and four years of marriage. Sophie Turner seemed like the first to move on after she was spotted with Peregrine Pearson in October, but Joe Jonas wasn’t far behind as he’s been spotted several times with rumored love interest, Stormi Bree.

Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner ‘are in competition’ to move on first

It wasn’t long after the divorce was announced to the public that the Game of Thrones star was seen engaging in PDA with Peregrine Pearson, British Aristocrat.

At the time, a source said that the Jonas Brother thought it was ‘too soon’ for her to be seen with someone else publically, but it wasn’t long before he followed suit.

He was seen on a ‘romantic ski trip’ with model Stormi Bree at the start of 2024, and just a couple of weeks ago, were spotted locking lips in Sydney.

 “They seem to have a competition going on,” a source told Life & Style. “Sophie’s telling friends she’s in love,” they continued. However, it looks like Joe may want to make the big move first.

“Joe’s keen on getting the divorce out. He would like to get to the altar first, no doubt about it,” the insider divulged to the publication.

Despite this, unlike Sophie, one source told US Weekly that Joe and Stormi “haven’t used the love word yet, but they’ve been seeing each other for a few months now and have been getting more serious over the past several weeks.”

Turner has also gone Instagram official with her new boo, but Joe and Stormi are yet to take this step.

Stormi is ‘part of the family’

The music star isn’t keeping the relationship too secret from the public, as he’s openly followed his new love interest on the ‘gram, and so has ‘could-be sister-in-law’ Priyanka Chopra.

The Bollywood actress even went one step further than just following the 33-year-old on the ‘gram, last month she ‘accidentally’ posted her.

In an Instagram story, which was swiftly deleted, Chopra innocently posted a photo of four hands clinking champagne glasses together, but internet detectives were one step ahead.

From the tattoos on her hand, they quickly worked out the glasses belonged to Stormi, Joe, Nick, and Priyanka.

The post was made just weeks after the new couple’s ‘first romantic trip’ to Aspen, so it looks like they weren’t ready for the hard launch just yet.
