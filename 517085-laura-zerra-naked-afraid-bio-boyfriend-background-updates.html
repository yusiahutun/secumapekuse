Laura Zerra's bio

The TV star was born on 16th Sep 1985 in Agawam, Massachusetts, USA. The American was born to her father, Steven Zerra, and mother, Elizabeth Zerra. While her mother is a preschool teacher, her father is an electrical engineer.

Growing up, Laura was fond of her father's adventurous nature, and they would venture into hunting trips together, which she enjoyed as a child. Laura grew up alongside her two sisters, one a doctor and the other a lawyer.

She attended one of her hometown high schools, after which the celeb enrolled at Connecticut College. She graduated, obtaining a degree in Ethnobiology. Unfortunately, the personality is yet to reveal the graduation year.

In 2004, Zerra joined the Buffalo Field Campaigns in West Yellowstone, Montana as an intern. While in the campaign, she was involved in documenting the slaughtering of the Bison wild herd, among other endangered species.

She moved to Vermont to join Roots School but ventured to Mexico in 2008. Laura started hitchhiking while honing her jungle survival skills, and in 2010 she decided to learn equine hoof care.

Reality TV star

In 2013, Laura Zerra was introduced to the entertainment industry by appearing in the Naked and Afraid Discovery TV show. She tested her survival skills for three weeks in Bocas Del Toro, Panama.

After that, Laura became a national star by appearing in several show episodes from 2013 to 2019. She remained with the production company working on other shows like:
