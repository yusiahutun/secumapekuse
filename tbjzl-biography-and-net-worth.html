TBJZL is a popular Youtube gamer channel run by Youtube star Tobi Brown, also known as Tobi Lerone, who posts videos and also streams gameplay using the broadcasting service Twitch. The first couple of videos he posted to his main channel TBJZL featured the video games Call of Duty, Fable II, and Minecraft. It wasn’t long until FIFA became his primary game of choice.

He has attracted more than 4.42 million subscribers to his YouTube channel with 473.6 million total views, making his channel ranked 2,948th subscribers and 19,707thh views as of March 2021. The most popular video on his channel is “TWO TOUCH CHALLENGE!!!” with 30.3 million views.

Tobi Brown was born on April 8, 1993, at Homerton University Hospital in London, England. He has a younger brother named Emmanuel John Brown (Manny). Tobi is considered to be one of the smartest Sidemen members. He graduated from Coventry University and got 7 A’s in his GCSE and a C in Latin.
