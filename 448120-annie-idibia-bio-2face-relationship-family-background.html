Annie Idibia's biography

Annie was born on November 13th, 1984, in Eket, Akwa Ibom, Nigeria. Unfortunately, a lot is unknown about her parents, except that they divorced when the actress was just 10 years. So, she and her young brothers went with their mother.

Being raised by a single mother alongside two brothers, Emmanuel and Wisdom, was a big challenge. Her mother had to keep moving to source a living. The family first moved to Jakande Estate, then to Thomas estate in Lekki, Lagos. Sadly, they lost their Emmanuel to tuberculosis.

Due to the hardship, Annie was compelled to look for work and support the family. Before she joined the university, she worked as a salesperson.

Educational background

The Nollywood actress epitomises beauty with brains. Despite her challenging early life, she made it to university. She holds two degrees; Computer Science from Lagos States University and Theatre Arts from the University of Lagos.

Profession

Annie's journey to fame has not been a walk in the park. She took many acting and modelling gigs that came her way. Even though she did not receive much pay for them, she never lost hope and kept working towards her goal.

The actress was catapulted to fame in 2004 through the video of 2Baba's hit African Queen. The video was popular at the time and gave her the taste of stardom. It also gave her the platform to launch her profession.

Before she ventured into acting, Annie participated in a beauty pageant called Queen of All Nations. She emerged as the second runner up. Annie Idibia made her movie debut in 2009.

She was featured in Pleasure and Crime and Blackberry Babes. These were her breakthrough roles, and she has never looked back. To date, she has acted in more than 10 films.
