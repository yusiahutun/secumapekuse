Mercedes Northup Job

We’ve already mentioned that Mercedes Northup worked four jobs before she appeared on The Bachelor. But what did she do? Well, she was a food server, a social media marketing intern, a case manager intern, and an online seller.

Talking in detail, she joined North Italia in Nashville, Tennessee as a food server in September 2019 and was working there even as of 2023. On the other hand, she only worked as a social media marketing intern at BRND House for 3 months (May 2022 – Jul 2022) and as a case manager intern at Nashville Angels for 6 months.

As for her time as an online seller, Mercedes has been selling different items on her Amazon shop for quite a while now. The items that she sold were listed under categories such as Valentine’s Day Gifts, Women’s Health, Valentine’s Day FIts, Home Decor, and Skincare Must-Haves.

Reportedly, she received a commission for each item she sold.

Also, from May 2020 to Nov 2020, Mercedes was a shopping specialist for Uncommon Jame, and from May 2018 to Mar 2020, she worked as an assistant at Meetings n moore.

By 2022, she garnered a net worth of under $250 thousand.
