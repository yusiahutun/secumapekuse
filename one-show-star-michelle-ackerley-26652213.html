The One Show star Michelle Ackerley announces engagement

 Posted on April 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  462 words  |  Martina Birk

The One Show star Michelle Ackerley flashed her diamond ring as she revealed her beau has popped the question.

The presenter told fans on Instagram that her partner Benjamin Ryan had proposed during a morning walk.

Sharing a snap of them smiling while she held up her hand to show off her sparkler, Michelle wrote: "Nipped out for a morning stroll when this happened…!"

Benjamin shared the same sweet snap on his own account and told his followers: "Popped out for a stroll and asked @michelle_ackerley to pop that onto her finger. Thankfully she said yes."

The pair have been together for some time and Michelle's fans said they were thrilled that she is to tie the knot.

Michelle is best known for The One Show, and has been presenting episodes since 2016.

She has also appeared on other television shows such as All Over The Place, Watchdog and This Morning.

The presenter, 37, has also appeared in Celebrity MasterChef.

She and Ben have been dating for many years.

In the past, Michelle opened up about her battle with endometriosis, and later started a hormone treatment for it.

She endured menopause symptoms at the age of 24 as a result of it.

Speaking to Closer, she said: "I was experiencing intense pain with my endometriosis and heavy bleeding.

"I’d had laparoscopies [keyhole surgery to investigate inside the abdomen] and had tried various treatments, including pain killers, over the years, when my gynaecologist suggested trying hormone replacement therapy injections."
