Oscars afterparty 2023: Show-stopping gowns from Sofia Vergara, Heidi Klum, Olivia Wilde &amp; more

 Posted on March 6, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  776 words  |  Larita Shotwell

After finishing the 2023 Oscars awards ceremony at the Dolby Theatre, some of the biggest stars went on to celebrate the winners at the Vanity Fair afterparty and Elton John's AIDS Foundation's viewing party.

And if you thought their champagne carpet gowns were incredible, just wait until you see the likes of Salma Hayek and Kate Hudson in their party outfits! Keep scrolling to see eye-catching sequins, figure-hugging silhouettes and more and remind yourself of their outfits earlier in the day in the video below…

WATCH: Oscars 2023 best red carpet looks

Riley Keough

Riley Keough turned heads in a gold cut-out gown covered with shimmery sequins and featuring a cowl neck and delicate straps. She wore her long hair in effortless waves, added Boucheron jewellery and accentuated her natural beauty with YSL Beauty products.

Kate Hudson

Brightening up the blue Vanity Fair carpet, Kate Hudson chose a fuchsia mermaid gown with a plunging neckline and oversized off-the-shoulder sleeves.

Lisa Rinna

Lisa Rinna was sure to turn heads in her deconstructed suit, with a white blazer-style top offering an off-the-shoulder look, a thigh-high leg split and long black gloves.

Gigi Hadid

A bold red colour, a slinky fitted silhouette, long sleeves and a leg split at the back – there's nothing we don't love about Gigi Hadid's latest look.

Salma Hayek

Salma Hayek may have changed out of her red sequins for the evening, but she still ensured she had a sparkling gown in the form of this silver and lace figure-hugging design with a low V-neck and leg split.

Heidi Klum

Yellow was the color of the moment for Heidi Klum, who opted for a bold feathered number from Georges Hobeika with long sleeves and a shorter skirt that highlighted her toned legs.

Sofia Vergara

Sofia Vergara glittered in gold as she posed for photos in a fitted sequin gown with a strapless neckline. She wore her hair in loose waves and wore red lipstick to up the glamour.

Kendall Jenner

Kendall Jenner showed off her model frame and opted for a gold scale-effect gown with a cross-over back and a figure-skimming silhouette.

Kerry Washington

With delicate spaghetti straps and long flowing cream material, Kerry Washington's archive Donna Karan Collection gown was effortlessly gorgeous.

Helena Christensen

Model Helena Christensen kept her look classic with a bespoke white V-neck gown by Saudi designer Atelier Hekayat, with production support Emilia Wickstead. It featured a fitted waist and mermaid skirt – perfection! 

Chrissy Teigen

Chrissy Teigen paired her new red hair with a heavily embellished green frock, complete with a low V-neck, a high leg split and feathered cuffs.

Olivia Wilde

Showing off a more daring side, Olivia Wilde's gown featured a one-shouldered neckline, leading to a draped waist that showed off her black leather bralette.

Hilary Duff

Looking gorgeous in red, Hilary Duff paired her polka-dot, full-skirted gown with a matching red lip, drop earrings and slicked-back hair.

Jessica Alba

Jessica Alba never fails to impress with her red (or in this case blue!) carpet outfits – how pretty is her strapless navy gown which she paired with a matching clutch and diamond choker?

Hailey Bieber

Hailey Bieber proved you can't go wrong with a classic black dress – and we're loving the modern shoulder pads and sleeve split.

Halle Berry

Halle Berry looked incredible in a strapless black bodysuit layered underneath a sheer A-line mini dress covered with organza bows.

Ashley Graham

Ashley Graham switched into a sheer bathrobe-like ensemble perfect for a Hollywood night out.

Billie Eilish

Billie never fails to bring out bold, glamorous, and voluminous looks to the red carpet.

Christina Aguilera

Christina Aguilera kept it simple yet sultry with a velvet and rhinestone look perfect for night time.

Ciara

Ciara was unafraid to bare it all and did so fabulously with her totally sheer gown.

Hunter Schafer

Hunter Schafer also didn't hesitate to bare it all, and seriously impressed in her Ann Demeulemeester ensemble.

Kate Beckinsale

Kate Beckinsale went for total Hollywood glamor and showed up with sequins and rhinestones galore, accessorising with YESSAYAN earrings.

Naomi Campbell

Ever the supermodel, Naomi Campbell looked chic as ever in her sequin, tropical-esque gown.

Sophie Turner

Sophie Turner opted for a little bit of sheer and loads of glitz for her chic look.

Paris Jackson

The daughter of the King of Pop, Paris Jackson, looked like a perfect emo princess in her tulle gown.
