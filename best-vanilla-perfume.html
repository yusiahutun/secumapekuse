16 Best Vanilla Perfumes of 2024

Best New Vanilla Scent

Snif Vanilla Vice

Best New Vanilla Scent

Snif Vanilla Vice

If walking into an ice cream parlor is one of your favorite smells in the world, you've found your new scent. It leans into the decadence of vanilla, but thanks to amber and musk isn't too cloying.

Notes:

Ice cream, Madagascar vanilla, jasmine sambac, amberwood, musk, and orcanox

One satisfied customer writes:

"This is no ordinary vanilla. It’s rich and warm and mysteriously beautiful. My new favorite!" —Leslie

Best Not-Too-Sweet Vanilla Scent

DS & Durga Deep Dark Vanilla

Best Not-Too-Sweet Vanilla Scent

DS & Durga Deep Dark Vanilla

Credit: LITTLE WOLF COLLECTIVE
Pros
  • Genderless

Vanilla as we know it is sweet, and often dessert-like—not so in this interpretation. Leaning more smoky and, as the name suggests, deep and dark, it has dimension and a unisex quality without abandoning vanilla altogether.

Notes:

  • Top: cabreuva, orchid, pink pepper
  • Heart: vines, green vanilla leaves, cypress root
  • Base: vanilla absolute, dark patchouli, hay

One satisfied customer writes:

"This is the most masculine vanilla scent I’ve ever put my nose on. It actually smells deep—low down and to the ground. It’s an incredibly earthy vanilla anchored by those hay and patchouli notes." —Stella

Best Spicy Vanilla Scent

Boy Smells Vanilla Era

Best Spicy Vanilla Scent

Boy Smells Vanilla Era

Pros
  • Layered and dimensional

You don't have to be in your vanilla era to love this fragrance, which also has notes of black pepper, saffron, and coffee absolute for a rounded, warm experience.

Notes:

  • Top: white tulip, black pepper, Madagascar frankincense, saffron
  • Heart: cashmere wood iso e super, beetroot, black amber
  • Base: vanilla absolute, coffee absolute, papyrus india

One satisfied customer writes:

"This perfume is like being wrapped in a sweet, warm and fragrant embrace. It turns heads and invites hugs!" —Cassandra

Best to Wear at Night

Byredo Night Veil Vanielle Antique Extrait de Parfum

Best to Wear at Night

Byredo Night Veil Vanielle Antique Extrait de Parfum

Credit: Nordstrom

It's easy to see—or smell—why Byredo is so popular, and Vanille Antique, one of the brand's night veil scents, doesn't disappoint. You might be expecting something super sweet with opening notes like freesia and plum, but the heart and base notes of amber and cashmere woods add an Earthy sophistication.

Notes

  • Top: Freesia, plum
  • Heart: Amber, cashmere woods
  • Base: Madagascar vanilla beans, cistus oil

One satisfied customer writes:

"I really, really, love this perfume. I adore the smell its a mixture of vanilla and has a musky undertone so its not too overwhelming or feminine. The perfume is a light and long lasting scent with absolutely gorgeous packaging and bottle that make it a beautiful piece even within itself —10/10 would recommend." —andy52

Best Citrus Vanilla Scent

Santa Maria Novella Vaniglia

Best Citrus Vanilla Scent

Santa Maria Novella Vaniglia

Credit: Smallflower

According to fragrance brand Santa Maria Novella, vanilla was actually thought of as an aphrodisiac in ancient times. Even so, the zippy lemon and bergamot, comforting jasmine, and grounding vanilla make it easy to fall in love with this fragrance.

Notes

  • Top: Lemon, bergamot, orange
  • Heart: Jasmine, vanilla
  • Base: Vanilla, sweet woods, benzoin

Best Floral Vanilla

YSL Beauty Black Opium Eau de Parfum

Best Floral Vanilla

YSL Beauty Black Opium Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Editor favorite

Though the name is Black Opium, this iconic fragrance is both sweet and warm thanks to notes of coffee, white flowers, and vanilla. It's the perfect one to pick up when that chill in the air comes around.

Notes

  • Top: Coffee
  • Heart: White flowers
  • Base: Vanilla

One satisfied customer writes:

"This is the best perfume I have purchased! Perfect for many different occasions. Definitely recommend!!!" —hannah1999g

Best Gourmand Vanilla

Dior Vanilla Diorama Fragrance

Best Gourmand Vanilla

Dior Vanilla Diorama Fragrance

Now 21% Off

Credit: Dior
Pros
  • Smells good enough to eat

This fragrance is named after one of Christian Dior's favorite desserts, the Diorama Gourmand (details of the actual pastry are a mystery as there are no true records of it). But this fragrance interpretation is just as delicious as the mythical dessert, with nearly-edible notes of vanilla, cocoa, and orange wedge.

Notes

  • Top: Vanilla
  • Heart: Cocoa
  • Base: Orange wedge

One satisfied customer writes:

"The fragrance is lovely, with a unique mix of florals and vanilla that offers a deepness to it." —Jenny

Best Celebrity-Approved Vanilla

Mugler Alien Goddess Eau de Parfum

Best Celebrity-Approved Vanilla

Mugler Alien Goddess Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Soft and warm

Bergamot and heart of jasmine are grounded by bourbon vanilla to create a warm yet floral scent you'll want to wear every day.

Notes

  • Top: Bergamot essence
  • Heart: Jasmine grandiflorum
  • Base: Bourbon vanilla

One satisfied customer writes:

"This is by far my favorite perfume of all time. It smells of vanilla and coconut and is king lasting." —amandajo45

Best Nostaglic Vanilla

By Rosie Jane Dulce Eau de Parfum

Best Nostaglic Vanilla

By Rosie Jane Dulce Eau de Parfum

Credit: by Rosie Jane
Pros
  • Carbon neutral

Fragrance has a way of bringing back memories, and this scent is all about reminiscing on all of the sweetness life has to offer. Tip: Because the formula is clean, spritz all over instead of rubbing or dabbing into skin.

Notes

  • Top: vanilla
  • Heart: hinoki wood
  • Base: nude musk

One satisfied customer writes:

"This scent is beautiful, feminine and airy! Every time I wear it, I get loads of compliments. I've been on the search for the perfect vanilla and always came up short until Dulce was released. It is now a signature scent for me, and I hope Rosie Jane never retires it." —Ashlyn

Heretic Dirty Vanilla Eau de Parfum

Best Scent for Layering

Heretic Dirty Vanilla Eau de Parfum

Credit: Bluemercury
Pros
  • Clean formula

Vanilla is the heart of this fragrance, blending a mix of both sweet and spicy as well as warm and woody to make an elevated vanilla scent. They call it dirty, we call it delicious.

Best Barely-There Vanilla

Chanel Paris-Venise Les Eaux de Chanel Eau de Toilette Spray

Best Barely-There Vanilla

Chanel Paris-Venise Les Eaux de Chanel Eau de Toilette Spray

Credit: Chanel
Pros
  • Gorgeous bottle

This fragrance is meant to evoke a sense of comfort. In fact, it was inspired by Venice, one of the cities Gabrielle Chanel fell in love with after the death of her lover, Boy Capel. Think of it as a fresh vanilla hug.

Notes

  • Heart: Amber, vanilla
  • Base: Tonka

Best Modern Vanilla

Jo Malone Vanilla & Anise Cologne

Best Modern Vanilla

Jo Malone Vanilla & Anise Cologne

Now 39% Off

Credit: Jo Malone
Pros
  • Makes a great gift

Hints of anise and clove add spiciness to the purple vanilla orchid notes in this cologne. It's meant to be a "modern story of vanilla."

Best Musky Vanilla

Ellis Brooklyn Vanilla Milk Eau de Parfum

Best Musky Vanilla

Ellis Brooklyn Vanilla Milk Eau de Parfum

Credit: Sephora
Pros
  • Biodegradable

This perfume does double duty with vanilla using both bourbon vanilla bean and Madagascar vanilla bean extract with a touch of florals. The best part? It's designed to get better with your body's heat and chemistry.

Notes

  • Top: creamy milk accord, frangipani, peony rose
  • Heart: bourbon vanilla bean, Madagascar vanilla bean extract, upcycled cocoa shell extract
  • Base: benzoin resinoid, amyris, sandalwood, musk

One satisfied customer writes:

"I’m literally just leaving the store after I randomly sprayed it on myself. I am in love. I came straight here to post a review LOL. For all my people who don’t like that child-like, dessert type vanilla smell, this scent is for you!" —Kenzie87

Best Spicy Vanilla

Diptyque Eau Duelle Eau de Toilette

Best Spicy Vanilla

Diptyque Eau Duelle Eau de Toilette

Credit: Bluemercury
Pros
  • Long-lasting

The sweetness of bourbon vanilla is accompanied by pink peppercorn, cardamom, and vetiver to add a warm spiciness to this take on a vanilla fragrance.

Notes

  • Top: Pink peppercorn, juniper berry
  • Heart: Green cardamom, black ceylon tea
  • Base: Bourbon vanilla, Haitian vetiver

One satisfied customer writes:

"This is quickly becoming my favorite scent. It is a spicy and sophisticated vanilla. It also lasts a long time. I spray on in the morning and can still smell at the end of the day. I will always repurchase!" —caseym55

Best Clean Vanilla

Henry Rose Dark is Night Eau de Parfum

One satisfied customer writes:

"Dark is Night is a gorgeous fragrance for any season." —wolff 1999
