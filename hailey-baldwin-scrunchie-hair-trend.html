Hailey Baldwin is bringing back the scrunchie

 Posted on June 18, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  120 words  |  Martina Birk

Getty Images

First they were on the catwalk at New York Fashion Week and now they’ve secured the celeb endorsement from model of the moment Hailey Baldwin. Yep, it’s safe to say scrunchies have made their comeback.

In contrast to the high fashion look, where the scrunchies hugged low, loose ponytails, Baldwin wore hers to secure a high, tight pony (also très on trend), and matched it to her air hostess-esque blue tuxedo dress.

Getty Images

The scrunchie is the latest in a long list of 90s beauty comebacks, following hippy hair wraps (showcased recently on Halle Berry), space buns, crimping and straight pin barrettes giving good Clueless vibes a la Bella Hadid.

What next? Butterfly clips? Surely not.
