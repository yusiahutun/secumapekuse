Meghan Markle Wore a Backless LBD For a Date With Harry

 Posted on August 30, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Meghan Markle's spring wardrobe, which includes white halter dresses, floral capes, and sheer blouses, has been all over our fashion mood boards. And her most recent look proved that a little black dress is a year-round staple. Over the weekend, Markle and her husband Prince Harry attended a dinner in Florida for Harry's organization Sentebale alongside polo player Ignacio Figueras.

In a series of images shared to Figueras and his wife Delfina Blaquie's Instagrams, Markle can be seen sitting alongside her husband in an asymmetric black backless St. Agni halter dress with unexpected lower-back cutouts paired with Mach & Mach shoes. Her accessories included black-and-gold Vintage Dior earrings and a gold bracelet, and her hair was slicked back into her signature low bun. Markle's smoky eyeshadow and dewy complexion perfectly complemented her LBD.

One photo in Figueras's roundup of the event, hosted at the Grand Champions Polo Club, captured the Sussexes talking with the famed polo player (Harry's longtime pal) and his wife while seated next to each other.

Earlier in the weekend, Harry and Figueras took to the pitch for a little friendly competition during the Royal Salute Polo Challenge, a yearly fundraiser benefitting Sentebale, which helps the youth in Africa. For the special polo match, Markle wore a striking summer-ready white halter dress from Heidi Merrick with a surprising keyhole cutout, bow detail, and pleated skirt.

Harry's team defeated the Grand Champions Team during the match, and Markle adorably presented them with the trophy before stealing a kiss from her husband.

The annual Royal Salute Polo Challenge came just days after it was announced that Archewell Productions would be producing two new Netflix shows, one of which will be centered around polo and this very event. People confirmed that this year's festivities were, in fact, filmed for the upcoming project.
