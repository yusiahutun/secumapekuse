Herms Kelly Mini Bag - Herms Releases Mini Kelly Bag

 Posted on April 21, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  201 words  |  Martina Birk

In the grand tradition of Goldilocks, Hermès has created the perfect Kelly. Just a little bigger than the covetable baby Kelly Micro and smaller than the beautifully classic Kelly, it's been made to fit all our modern contraptions—smart phones included—while maintaining its petite insouciance. It may not be able to be utilized the same way it got its name (Grace Kelly famously held the bag over her body in photos to conceal her baby bump in the 1950s), but we love it just the same. Modern baby bellies are for flaunting, anyway.

The Hermès Kelly Mini II retails for approximately $8,150, hermes.com for details.
