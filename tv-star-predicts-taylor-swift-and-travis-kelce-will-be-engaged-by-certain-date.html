TV star predicts Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce will be engaged' by certain date

 Posted on April 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  380 words  |  Martina Birk

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s relationship seems to be going from strength to strength, so much so that Hilarie Burton believes they’ll be engaged by May.

Like the rest of the internet, the TV star saw their adorable PDA at Taylor’s concert in Argentina this weekend, and she’s fangirling as much as the rest of us.

Hilarie Burton predicts Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce’s engagement date

Burton, known for her role in One Tree Hill, joined the Swifties over the weekend as she gushed over Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce‘s budding relationship.

Taking to X, formerly known as Twitter on Sunday after the concert, she wrote: “They are gonna have the most flannel lined, pie and red ribbon kinda Christmas….and then these babies are gonna be engaged by May.”

The tweet was written in response to the NFL star’s reaction to Taylor changing her lyrics for him. Whilst performing her hit song Karma, Swift sent the crowd, and her ‘boyfriend’ crazy as she sang:

“Karma is the guy on the Chiefs coming straight home to me.”

Responding to Burton’s tweet, one Swiftie said: “Ok I guess it’s for real, she’s never done that for any other guy.”

She then went on to gush over the couple even more, as she claimed “every other guy made her feel like she had to downplay her own work.”

“This big ol beefy tree of a man is holding up signs and singing along! Catching her as she runs offstage?!?!

“Just saying….strong @JDMorgan energy. Get a man who lights up when you win,” the actress wrote.

The NFL star heads back to the US after supporting his ‘partner’ in Argentina

After flying 5701 miles from Kansas City to Argentina, the Chiefs player has now returned back home.

The star was spotted boarding a plane on Sunday morning, less than 24 hours after watching Taylor rock the stage in Buenos Aires.

He landed in the South American country on Friday and was seen out with Taylor’s father, Scott Swift.

The two were standing together at the concert, so it looks like Kelce is in with the family. Travis’ father is very fond of Taylor too, as he recently told PEOPLE:

“She’s a very, very sweet, very charming, down-to-earth young woman.”
