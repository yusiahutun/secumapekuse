There’s more to DJing than spinning tunes, mixing, and adding samples and filters. If you’re the type of DJ that loves to have complete control over the show, you need a good lighting setup, too.

Having the right set of lights can enhance any show, whether you pre-program the display or set the lights to sound-activation mode. You may have a mobile DJ setup for weddings and events or play shows in bars or clubs. Whatever the occasion, energizing the set with the proper lighting is essential to keeping the crowd engaged.

Here's a look at some of the best DJ lights for gigs and parties available today.

FAQ

Q: What Are the Best DJ Lights?

DJ lights have evolved so much over the decades that there is now a vast range of different lights offering a multitude of effects. Choosing the best DJ lights really comes down to the events you will use them for and your personal preference.

Another important aspect to consider is your budget. You won't need anything too powerful and expensive for house parties and intimate bar or club gigs. But, for larger events, such as a wedding party, you’ll need to consider things like wattage and beam angle.

Q: Why Should a DJ Have Lights?

The right music is absolutely essential to a successful gig. However, to create different feels and atmospheres, DJs can utilize lighting to enhance and energize their mixes. Electronic music DJs may use strobes and lasers to help build the music up to a visual crescendo. The idea is to inspire the audience to move their bodies, become part of the event, and remain on the dance floor.

For events such as weddings, the lighting may not be as vital. However, it still creates a much-needed ambiance for enhancing the DJ's musical output.
