Demi Moore Gives the Sheer Trend a Dazzling Twist in a Sequin Gown

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  307 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The celebrity-loved sheer trend just got a stamp of approval from Demi Moore—who added her own dazzling flair.

The Ghost star attended Dolce & Gabbana’s 40th anniversary party Saturday in Milan, looking like a goddess. Moore was captivating in a see-through, floor-length black gown that featured a column construction, long sleeves, and a high scoop neckline, and was completely covered in glittering sparkles. She radiated in the piece—with the sequins dancing about as they caught the light—which she wore over a black bra and matching high-waisted briefs, paired with strappy black open-toe heels. She let the brilliant frock shine, eschewing jewelry completely, and wore pretty, fresh makeup, complete with a natural-pink lip and dark mascara. Her luscious dark locks served as the perfect accessory to her look, which she styled straight with a middle part, cascading down to her hips.

Robino Salvatore

The actor-producer first dabbled in the sheer trend in February, when she put a saucy spin on a black blazer dress. She appeared on The Late Show With Stephen Colbert to promote her new FX series, Feud: Capote vs. the Swans, in a double-breasted blazer minidress from Versace. The piece featured peak lapels, front pockets, satin fabric buttons, and, for a flirty touch, panels of sheer black lace mesh along the bodice and back. She completed the look with all-black accents, including sheer black tights, glossy slingback heels from Giuseppe Zanotti with a diamond-adorned strap, and dangly silver earrings. For a final accessory, she added her adorable tiny Chihuahua, Pilaf, who accompanied Moore throughout her Feud engagements.

Her small furry friend was noticeably and unfortunately missing from her trip to Italy.
