Shahs Of Sunset star Lilly Ghalichi's Los Angeles home burglarized while on vacation in St. Bart's

 Posted on August 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  548 words  |  Martina Birk

The Los Angeles home of Lilly Ghalichi - who previously starred on Bravo reality series Shahs Of Sunset - has been burglarized while on a family vacation in St. Bart's.

The property owned by the 38-year-old lawyer - full name Neelufar Seyed 'Lilly' Ghalichi -  and husband Dara Mir was broken into on Friday while they were abroad, TMZ reported on Monday morning.

The break-in seemingly came at a convenient time, however, many celebrities have had their homes burglarized in recent years after documenting their trips on on social media.

Law enforcement sources told the publication that suspects could have noticed her recent postings from St. Bart's then targeted her home.

The insiders also say that there are three suspects in the case - two men and one woman - who dressed in all black including beanies.

It is alleged that the two men had broken windows at the home while the woman served as the lookout.

Police sources for TMZ said that when the burglars had broken a window in the master closet in attempt to gain entry, the alarm was triggered. 

If there is a bit of good news, it was that the alarm sent the group running from the property as they were unable to take anything from the home.

The trio of suspects had reportedly got away on foot while neighborhood security and policed chased them including use of a police helicopter.
