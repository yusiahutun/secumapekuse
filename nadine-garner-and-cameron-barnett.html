About

Nadine Garner is a 52 year old Australian Actress. Born Nadine Lynette Garner on 14th December, 1970 in Knoxfield, Melbourne, Victoria, Australia, she is famous for her roles as Rachel Burns in the long-running Seven Network/Network Ten/10 Peach soap opera Neighbours, Tamara "Tammy" Henderson in the Network Ten drama series The Henderson Kids, Deborah "Deb" Callum in the ABC1/Nickelodeon drama series Blue Water High, Detective Senior Constable Jennifer Mapplethorpe in the Seven Network police drama series City Homicide, and Jean Beazley in the ABC/Seven Network mystery drama series The Doctor Blake Mysteries in a career that spans 1985–present.
