MAFS UK's April Banbury, 33, wows in tiny bikini as 2022 series launch date confirmed

 Posted on July 21, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  563 words  |  Martina Birk

Married At First Sight UK has revealed its exciting 2022 line up ahead of the second series of the show's UK edition.

One of the brides, April Banbury, ironically works as a bridal dress shop owner and is looking for 'the one' on the show.

Since announcing her place on the show, fans have been going wild at the shop-owner's stunning body, which she regularly flaunts to her 24.4 thousand Instagram followers.

Proven to be a sun-chaser in a post from October 2021, April wrote she was feeling "beyond positive" whilst lounging on a daybed in sunny Marbella, showing off her amazing body in a graceful sea-green bikini.

Posting the snap, April added a caption which went into detail about her struggles with eating disorders when she was growing up and now works as a proud ambassador for SEED charity, a eating disorders support service.

In the caption of the bikini pic, she wrote: "You may look at this photo a think I look like I have no worries, but let me tell you it took me a long time to get to this point where I feel comfortable enough in my own skin and to speak out about my story."

"I genuinely believe that going though my battle with anorexia as a child has led me to this point. I am now able to help those who need it the most," she added.

She concluded her message, saying: "I lost years of my life battling with this illness in my own head because I didn’t understand, or know what it was. It’s time to educate everyone."

April's beauty and support work was solidified when she was crowned Miss Great Britain in 2020, a title she held for 18 months due to Covid. Her winner's dress, a stunning, elegant emerald green gown, was one she designed and created herself.

In the post celebrating her two-year anniversary as Miss GB, April wrote: "I feel I still made the most of every opportunity, not forgetting my proudest moment becoming a SEED ambassador helping people all the UK suffering with eating and mental health disorders."

Married At First Sight UK is set to air on E4 on Monday, August 29
