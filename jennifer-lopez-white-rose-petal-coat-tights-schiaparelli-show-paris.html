Jennifer Lopez Covered Herself in White Rose Petals for Schiaparelli

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  366 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Here to remind us of our deep love of dramatic, head-turning fashion is Schiaparelli.

The fashion house debuted its Spring/Summer 2024 haute couture collection in Paris today, and filled its front row with some of the world’s biggest names—including Zendaya and Jennifer Lopez.

For the occasion, J.Lo brought the romance and the chaos with her head-to-toe Schiaparelli look. The superstar looked incredible in an oversize coat covered all over in fresh white rose petals and embellished with a silver sequined sun on the back.

“A coat made of 7,000 real rose petals kept fresh and alive by sugar water, also known as hummingbird nectar. Designed especially for me by @DanielRoseberry for @Schiaparelli Couture 2024. Excited for this journey into my Hearts + Flowers Era…#ThisIsMeNow coming February 16,” Lopez wrote in an Instagram post featuring photos of the look.

She layered the statement piece over a ribbed white turtleneck sweater adorned with silky cream strands that crawled up her abdomen like fancy octopus arms. For bottoms, she opted for opaque black tights, which she cinched at the waist with a chunky beige belt with golden accents.

Peter White//Getty Images

Lopez finished the look with black velvet heels with a golden lock detail at the front—matching the golden lock charm on her belt—plus chunky gold earrings and surrealist golden sunglasses made to look like squinty alien eyes with striking eyebrows. She carried Schiaparelli’s Anatomy jewelry bag—a sophisticated, couture take on Mrs. Potato Head, if you ask us.

Peter White//Getty Images

Lopez wore bronzy makeup and debuted a new hairdo—a chic bob styled into a slicked-back wet look, with strands of hair framing her face. Zendaya also showed off a new hairdo at the show, her signature dark brown locks transformed by choppy microbangs, which framed her forehead and added to the futuristic vibe of her ensemble.
