If there’s one shoe we’ll never regret adding to our wardrobe, it’s a classic combat boot. A quality lug sole design can toughen up and enhance almost any outfit, and the good news is, you don’t need to spend big in order to score a cute pair. We’ve found tons of Prada combat boot lookalikes, all of which are nearly identical to the designer option that stars like Kendall Jenner, Kourtney Kardashian, Lori Harvey, and Nina Dobrev love. Even better? Our favorites ring in well under $100.

Ahead, you’ll find combat boots that look and feel luxe, but come with a price tag that’s a bit easier on the bank account. Many of these picks are worthy alternatives to Prada’s Monolith boot, but we’ve also included a handful of simpler styles, as well as one that could pass for the brand’s 110mm boots, which are a Taylor Swift favorite.

  • Cape Robbin MonaLisa Combat Boots

    Colors Available: 9

    Size Range: 6 – 11

    Two details that make Prada’s combat boots stand out from the pack are the lug sole and the side pouch — and this alternative has both. Plus, customers say that, despite the suspiciously reasonable price tag, they’re actually decent quality.

    “You never know with Amazon, but I’m very happy with these boots,” reads one review. “They are super comfortable for my wide-width feet and seem to be well made. Until I’m rich enough to buy the Prada Monoliths, these are a good stand-in!”

  • Juicy Couture Women's Quentin Combat Boots

    Colors Available: 2

    Size Range: 6.5 – 10

    Juicy Couture may be famous for its tracksuits, but the brand should get some credit for its Quentin combat boots, too. These shoes give off that same, slightly rugged yet luxe vibe as Prada’s version, but are way easier on the wallet at just $75.

  • Steve Madden Kenny Lug Sole Bootie

    Colors Available: 9

    Size Range: 6 – 11

    Steve Madden has never failed to provide us with shockingly good alternatives to cute designer styles, and this time, it’s no different. The Kenny boot is essentially the perfect mix of Prada’s Monolith design and its Brushed-leather boots, combining a timeless look with a thicker sole.

  • Free People Jones Lug Sole Lace Up Boots

    Colors Available: 3

    Size Range: 6 – 11

    The best part about this Prada combat boots dupe is that, while there are plenty of similarities, it also has features that make it feel unique. Customers specifically love the proportions — the ankle is slim and form-fitting while the bottom of the boot is chunky.

    “I get sooo many compliments when I wear these,” says one review. “A bit heavy bc of the lug sole but not uncomfortably so. I have skinny ankles and like that you can tighten the shaft for a really snug fit. Fav boots of the season.”

  • Mix No. 6 Ariee Combat Boot

    Colors Available: 1

    Size Range: 6 – 11

    Who knew it was possible to find such cute boots for under $30? Mix No. 6 pretty much nailed the luxury look with this eye-catching design, which features a slight platform and a cool buckle embellishment. You also won’t have to worry about your feet aching mere minutes after you slip these babies on. Like the description says, the footbed is lightly padded, which is a comfort-enhancing detail we’ll be extra thankful for at the end of the day.

    $24.989072% Off

  • Vince Camuto Repla Bootie

    Colors Available: 1

    Size Range: 6 – 11

    If your number one requirement for combat boots is versatility, you’ll want to check out Vince Camuto’s Repla Bootie. The classic lace-up style includes a bit of flare, thanks to the addition of a buckle, and they’re made by a tried-and-true footwear brand (everyone from JLo to Hailey Bieber is a fan). While customers warn that the shoe runs big, they also sing its praises, saying that the design is super comfy, well-made, and not as bulky as other options out there.

  • Soda Breach Combat Bootie With Side Zipper & Pouch

    Colors Available: 2

    Size Range: 6 – 12

    Despite looking cute, many of the pouches seen on combat boots are too small to hold anything substantial. But that’s not the case with this design from Soda , where the pouch isn’t just useful but also removable if you wish.

    “I love these boots. They are stylish and durable,” says one positive review. “I left one pouch on my left boot for keys, money and ID. Perfectly secure as I walked. I’d totally purchase these again in another color.”

  • GUESS Women's Tanisa Ankle Boot

    Colors Available: 2

    Size Range: 6 – 10

    For those looking for a dressier alternative, there are always heeled Prada combat boots. But rather than dropping over $1,000 on a single pair, Guess’ Tansia boot is a worthy replacement and costs less than $70. Not only is this shoe sturdy and chunky in all the right places, it also looks super similar to the boots that Taylor Swift recently wore to a Chiefs game.
