Kenyan-Mexican actress Lupita Nyong'o's brother has wowed netizens with his choice of fashion sense after stepping out, looking dapper for Black Panther's premier.

Lupita Nyong'o's brother's red carpet vibe

Fans of the superhero film had been waiting for the premier date and crossing their fingers that they wouldn't push it forward as they had done in the past.

As soon as the show's trailer was dropped, it was clear that everyone was patiently waiting to see the sequel without Chadwick Boseman, who was the original Black Panther.
