OnlyFans star impresses on Floyd Mayweather undercard after donning skimpy weigh-in outfit

 Posted on August 2, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  345 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A blonde bombshell impressed after turning up to her weigh-in dressed in nothing but a blue bikini top and joggers.

Sammy-Jo Luxton made her boxing debut on the Floyd Mayweather vs Aaron Chalmers undercard in London on Saturday and turned a few heads with her performance in the exhibition match. The 23-year-old has turned her hand to boxing after announcing earlier this month that she had signed with the Professional Fighters League.

Despite being a hotshot in MMA, Muay Thai and now boxing, the sexy fighter keeps fans involved as she continues to rake it in through OnlyFans.

Known as the "Ghetto Cinderella", Luxton took to the ring as she took on fellow boxing novice and MMA star Hayley Barraclough, 32, in a four-round exhibition match at the O2.

Luxton continued to land punch after punch as they battled it out in the ring with Barraclough walking away looking bruised and battered while Luxton celebrated her debut.

Tweeting after the fight, she said: "Can’t stop me with a team like this. Boxing debut at the o2 completed in styleeeee."

Lexton originally made her name in Muay Thai, with the fighter picking up a world silver medal as well as British and European titles in the sport before going pro in MMA.

Posting on her social media, she said earlier this month: "Best kept secret - I’ve signed to PFL! I’ve made the switch to MMA and I have been grinding away at @McrTopTeam - I will be making my debut in the smartcage later this year. Can’t wait to show the world what I’m made of."
