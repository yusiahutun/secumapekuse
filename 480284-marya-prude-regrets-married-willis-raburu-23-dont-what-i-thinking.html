Media personality Willis Raburu's ex-wife Marya Prude has revealed that she regrets getting married at the age of 23.

Raburu's ex-wife Marya says she wasted her time in marriage

Marya got married to Raburu in 2017 in a colourful wedding event, but in early 2020, they divorced a few months after losing their unborn child.

The 28-year-old shared a post talking about her past relationship, saying marriage is overrated.
