Salma Hayek is one of Hollywood’s hottest and most talented actresses. Here are some of her best photos.

Salma Hayek is widely considered one of the most talented and sexiest actresses in Hollywood. Salma made a name for herself after she moved to Tinseltown in the early ’90s. The actress had already garnered fame in Mexico as the titular character in the telenovela, ‘Teresa’. Her debut Hollywood role was in 1995’s ‘Desperado’ (pictured here) alongside Antonio Banderas. After that, Salma was a hot commodity for tons of roles.

Her other prominent films include, ‘From Dusk till Dawn’ (1996),’Wild Wild West’, and ‘Dogma’ (both 1999), ‘Frida’ (2002), ‘Grown Ups’ (2010), ‘Puss in Boots’ (2011), ‘Grown Ups 2’ (2013), ‘Tale of Tales’ (2015), ‘The Hitman’s Bodyguard’ (2017) and ‘Like a Boss’ (2020).
