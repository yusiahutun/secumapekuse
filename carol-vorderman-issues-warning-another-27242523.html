Carol Vorderman issues warning as another kn*b fakes her identity on Instagram

 Posted on July 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  396 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Carol Vorderman has been targeted by yet another imposter who has set up a fake Instagram account in her name.

The 61-year-old issued a stark warning to her fans, reminding them not to fall for the fake account.

Carol shared a screenshot of the profile to her 225,000 followers, captioning the post: "Fake account warning.

"Thank you for reporting another kn*b end for setting up this fake account. It gets boring after a while.

"Meanwhile proper life is bloody marvellous. Some amazing things going on. Booooooooommmmm."

She added "Kn*b End Banquet Hall for Kn*b Jockeys" as the location for her post.

It's not the first time the Countdown star has been targeted by fake accounts and she often exposes them as a thoughtful warning to her dedicated fans.

She has previously branded the posers as "pathetic".

Carol's fans flooded the star with supportive comments following her latest ordeal. One person said: "I hope it gets taken down soon, this makes me so angry."

Another fan wrote: "Sorry to hear Carol. I did have a message from someone saying they were Rod Stewart on TikTok."

One person questioned why people do it, while another said: "I am shocked how many there are. Money is the driver and it’s the root of all evil. They always ask for money. I don’t know why Instagram allow it?"

Carol likes to keep her fans posted on the good and the bad on social media and recently showed off her new blonde hair.

She posted a series of makeup-free selfies to reveal her new look. Carol was enjoying a trip to Wales and joked that her hair had been bleached by the sun.

She said: "Sun so hot it turned my hair blonde today ... didn't it @kalas_hair_boutique???"

Carol also recently appeared at the Top Gun: Maverick premiere, describing the film as "a-mazing".
