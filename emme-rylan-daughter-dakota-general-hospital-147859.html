General Hospital Star Emme Rylan Loves Sharing Pics of Daughter Dakota

 Posted on January 1, 0001  |  2 minutes  |  254 words  |  Martina Birk

Recently, General Hospital star Emme Rylan (aka Lulu) Instagrammed another beautiful photo of her five-month-old daughter, Dakota Rose, apologizing for the onslaught of baby pics. “I will stop eventually, I promise," she joked. Well, we for one hope she never does! Dakota is absolutely adorable and we can’t get enough of her! More, more, more, we say!

Emme gave birth to her third child with partner Don Money back on June 26, 2016. The pair has two sons together, Jackson and Levi. Almost a week later, the couple revealed the name they’d chosen for their first daughter. And luckily, the actress’s time off from General Hospital to give birth coincided with the soap’s scheduled three-week break in July, so she had a full month to enjoy her baby girl before going back to work!

The happy mommy has happily been posting photos of Dakota to her Instagram page, sharing the family’s adventures with the new addition. Emme has dressed her in an elephant costume and tutus, put bows on her head (and… a paper cup?!), and even turned her into a kitty via a Snapchat filter! Every picture of Dakota is cuter than the last, and we love watching her grow up before our very eyes, so hopefully, Emme doesn’t follow through on her promise and keeps sharing her family photos with us for a very long time!
