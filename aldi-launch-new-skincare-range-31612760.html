Aldi launch new skincare range similar to La Mer with prices starting from 5.99

 Posted on June 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  406 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Aldi are set to release a new luxury skincare range that's rival to luxury beauty brand La Mer, with prices starting from £5.99.

The supermarket's skincare and beauty brand Lacura are launching La Soie, which translates to "silk" in English, that includes a supercharged concentrate face serum, nourishing face cream, and renewing eye concentrate.

Lucky fans who have received a gift box prior the launch said the green, pink and gold packaging reminded them of the expensive brand La Mer, which is favoured by celebrities like Selena Gomez, Blake Lively and Gwyneth Paltrow.

One influencer called the jar of La Soie facial moisturiser "pure luxury in a pot".

Formulated with ingredients like algae extract and macadamia oil, the 30ml face cream helps lift and firm the appearance of textures while diminishing signs of ageing and soothing dryness.

It's priced at £6.99 — 95% cheaper than the legendary Creme de La Mer moisturiser, which retails at £155 for a 30ml bottle.

Shoppers can save more if they buy the concentrate serum, which is also available to buy for £6.99, and they can save a whopping £358 comparing to the La Mer concentrate serum.

"It smells like the most beautiful, luxurious spa in the world," another said while trying on the serum.

And the eye concentrate, which includes a small metal massage roller, is retailed for £5.99.

La Mer is known for its patented Miracle Broth, which they claimed, is crafted with a blend of sustainably harvested Giant Sea Kelp, vitamins, minerals and other nutrients.

With the huge price difference, it's hard to believe that La Soie copied the exact formula from La Mer but the eye concentrate "promises to brighten and revive the skin for noticeable, long-lasting results".

It's formulated with algae, lime and coffee extracts — which can reduce the appearance of dark circles and wrinkles around the delicate eye area.

Aldi's La Soie range is expected to be a sell-out when they are released in store on Sunday, December 10.
