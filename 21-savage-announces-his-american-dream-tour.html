21 Savage Announces His American Dream Tour

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  453 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

ATLANTA (CelebrityAccess) — Following his appearance on Saturday Night Live this weekend, the Grammy-winning rapper 21 Savage announced plans for his “American Dream” North American tour.

Produced by Live Nation, the tour is scheduled to begin May 1st at Rogers Arena in Vancouver, with 2p additional shows slated for North America before wrapping with a hometown performance at Lakewood Amphitheatre in Atlanta on June 15th.

Along the way, the tour is scheduled to hit major markets such as Los Angeles, Dallas, Houston, Chicago, and New York, where 21 Savage is scheduled to perform at the Governors Ball Music Festival.

The “American Dream Tour” is in support of 21 Savage’s third studio album of the same name.

Tickets will be available starting with an artist presale & VIP packages on February 28th, followed by a general onsale on March 1st.
