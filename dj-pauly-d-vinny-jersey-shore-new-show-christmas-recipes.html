OK, let's skip right to dessert! Here's DJ Pauly D's mom's tasty recipe for butter ball cookies. (DJ Pauly D, meanwhile, just scored an amazing Christmas gift: MTV announced today that it closed a series option deal with the star and was picking up the pilot he shot in September for his own show!).

Butter Ball Cookies

1 cup of butter
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped walnuts
4 tbsp confectioners sugar
2 cups sifted flour

Instructions:

-Cream butter, add sugar, continue to beat until light.
-Add vanilla and flour and mix well. Fold in nuts.
-Shape into small round balls on ungreased baking sheet.
-Bake at 350 degrees in oven for 15-18 minutes. Until bottom is light brown.
-When cooled, roll into confectioners sugar.
