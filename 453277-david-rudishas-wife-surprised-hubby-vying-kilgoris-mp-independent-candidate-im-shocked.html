David Rudisha's Wife Surprised Hubby is Vying for Kilgoris MP as Independent Candidate: &quot;I'm Shocked

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  392 words  |  Larita Shotwell
  • David Rudisha's wife, Lizzy Naanya, was in disbelief following reports that her husband was vying for the Kilgoris parliamentary seats
  • Lizzy's reaction came hours after it was reported that the Olympian would be seeking to be the constituency's next representative
  • Rudisha has been cleared by the Independent and Electoral Boundaries Commission (IEBC) to vie for the seat on an independent ticket

Olympian David Rudisha's wife has said she was astonished by the news that her husband would be vying for the Kilgoris parliamentary seat.

Lizzy Naanyu declared that she was surprised by her husband's decision to vie for the position on an independent ticket.

In a post shared on Facebook on Monday, May 2, on her account, Naanyu implied that she was not privy to the plans by Rudisha to seek an elective seat.

Rudisha cleared

In a clearance letter by the Independent and Electoral Boundaries Commission (IEBC), the 800m world record holder has been cleared to vie for the Kilgoris seat.

The letter further ould reveals that the Olympian doesn't subscribe to any political party and that he would vie as an independent candidate.

"Reference is made to the subject matter and your letter dated 29 April 2022 on the intention to contest election as Member of National Assembly, Kilgoris Constituency. Pursuant to Article 85 of the Constitution and Section 34(fa) of the Political Parties Act 2011, David Lekuta Rudisha, is not a member of any registered party."

Rudisha shows off magnificent living room

In a separate report, the renowned athlete opened up his home for the world to see with a photo of himself chilling out in his simple yet classy living room.
