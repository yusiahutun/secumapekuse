Salma Hayek celebrates billionaire father-in-law for being 'guiding light' in her life

 Posted on April 12, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  350 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Salma Hayek has taken to her Instagram to wish her father-in-law François Pinault a happy birthday, as she calls him her “guiding light”.

The Hollywood actress has been married to François Pinault’s son, François-Henri Pinault since 2009 at a gala at the Palazzo Grassi in Venice, three years prior.

Salma Hayek honors Father-in-law with an adorable birthday tribute

Taking to her 26 million Instagram followers, Salma Hayek penned an emotional birthday message to her French billionaire businessman François Pinault on his 87th birthday.

In the picture shared, the two can be seen clinking glasses as they beam with smiles at one another.

In the caption of the post, the movie star praised the billionaire’s wisdom and kindness, as she penned:

“Happy birthday to the man who raised the love of my life. Your wisdom, kindness, and warmth have been a guiding light in our lives. Cheers to laughter, love, and many more cherished moments together.”

The post comes after Salma has been providing her followers with tons of bikini snaps in Mexico, as she’s seen living her best life sipping margaritas by the sea. The dream.

Fans praise the star’s relationship with her in-laws

Salma’s Instagram followers took to the post to wish her father-in-law a happy birthday, as well as comment on how much they admire the relationship between the two.

“How wonderful it is to have an appreciative and caring relationship with in-laws,” penned one follower.

“Let me tell you how confused this post made me because I honestly thought that was @joebiden,” wrote one confused fan.

“He is such a wonderful and sweet man! Happy birthday Monsieur!” exclaimed actress Hélène de Fougerolles.

François Pinault is one of the richest men in the world

François Pinault is the honorary chairman of the luxury group Kering, which owns fashion brands Saint Laurent, Alexander McQueen, and Gucci, as per Forbes.

He founded the company in 1963, which first started off as a wood and building materials company, before changing the direction of the business in 1999.

The publication currently states his net worth to be $37.4 Billion.
