Lindsey Reiser- Professional Career

Lindsey began her career in journalism after completing her studies, working at KTSM/KDBC in El Paso, Texas, where she covered border and immigration issues. She also exposed poor corporate practices, reported on state corruption, and often highlighted cross-border violence. She moved to Arizona in 2010 and began working as a multimedia journalist for ComCorp of America, before joining Meredith Corporation as a Weekend anchor and reporter in August 2011. Lindsey had been with the company for more than 8 years before joining the MSNBC team.

Lindsey started working as a weekend anchor at MSNBC in New York, United States, in January 2020. She co-anchors Gibson & Reiser Report on weekends at MSNBC with Kendis Gibson, and she has been with the network for more than a year and a half as of May 2021. Her honors include the Mark of Excellence Award from the Society of Professional Journalists, the Heart Journalist Award, and multiple National Broadcast Education Association Awards.

Lindsey also did podcasts, the first of which was titled Wiser With Reiser and was released in August 2018 and focused on leveraging physical, mental, and financial wellness to cultivate a life well-lived. She started recording in August of 2018 and continued until January of 2020. Lindsey can now be seen on MSNBC’s Gibson & Reiser Report on weekends, and she is expected to cover more stories and reports in the future.
